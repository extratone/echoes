Registration for the Voyage Ceremony!

2022-07-21

In New Eden, reconstruction of the first stargate linking Amarr with Hedin marked the end of the long dark ages. 
It has been 2,070 years since that date. A voyage ceremony will be held in memory of the historic event.
An infinite amount of stars puts into perspective how small Pilots are, yet greatness exists within them.
Sail away, to the stars, to the battlefield, you have the blessing of all.
 
Attention Corp. CEOs and Director, registration for the Voyage Ceremony will be available soon and we are inviting all Corporations to join us for this event. Please check this registration guide:
1. If you intend to register for the Voyage Ceremony, please make sure that your Corporation meets the following requirements.
a. The CEO and Director have Omega Clone activated
b. Over 30% of the corporation's members have Omega Clone activated (rounding down)
c. Established more than 30 days ago
d. Over 30 active members
2. The top 3 corporations in the previous Inscription of the Galaxy rankings will be invited to participate, if they refuse to participate or do not meet the participation requirements, they not be included in the ranking
The Corporation invited in this way must meet the following conditionsÔºö
a. The CEO and Director have Omega Clone activated
b. Over 50% of the corporation's members have Omega Clone activated (rounding down)
c. Established more than 30 days ago
d. Over 50 active members in the last 30 days
3. Please note that the registration fee is 1000 AUR and will be fully refunded if the registration is unsuccessful.
4. The deadline for registration is: 07/28/2022 12AM (UTC-4)
Please note that due to space constraints, the maximum number of participants for this Voyage Ceremony is 50 Corporations, of which the list of non-invited corporations will be confirmed through random selection on the second day after the closing date, and will be announced separately.
Pilots who joined the Voyage Ceremony will receive a commemorative medal after the event.
In addition, Corporation banners that participated in the event will be permanently documented on the Voyage Ceremony memorial architecture. Every pilot visiting the universe will read the interspace epic poem written by the pioneers of the event.
