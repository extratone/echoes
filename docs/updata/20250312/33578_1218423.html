Patch Notes 0312

2025-03-12

To New Eden Capsuleers,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 3/12/2025 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.
 
New Features
Nanocore Data Bank
Capsuleers with the Omega Duo Bundle can use the Nanocore Data Bank. When Capsuleers with the Omega Duo Bundle dismantle a Nanocore, it will automatically be uploaded to the Data Bank. They can also manually upload cores from the station or citadel hangars or download them from the Data Bank to these locations.
Capsuleers can directly replace Nanocores that have been uploaded to the Data Bank in the Nanocore interface of any station or citadel. If a Capsuleer no longer possesses the Omega Duo Bundle, they will lose access to the Data Bank and will be unable to replace cores within it, but they can download them to the current station or citadel hangar.
 
Capsuleer Personal Office Inventory
The station operation panel has added the option to rent backup inventory in stations for categorizing and storing Capsuleers' items. All Capsuleers can rent, but only those with the Omega Duo Bundle can use the rented hangar.
Materials from the backup inventory will not be spent when producing with Construction or upgrading nanocores with modules.
Each station's backup inventory requires a weekly maintenance fee and rent of 4,000,000 ISK, which will be automatically deducted from the wallet.
When unable to pay the fee, the backup inventory will cease operation, and the materials inside will be frozen and cannot be moved in, moved out, or used until the fee is paid.
The overdue fee cap for each station's Personal Office Inventory is 4,000,000 ISK, and it does not accumulate over time.
  
Balance Adjustments
Ships
CyanSea. New bonuses: +50% Merit from defeating Pirates, 20% Insurance Claim discount, and gain damage and EHP bonuses from raising in rank (same as other CONCORD ships).
Venture Covert Ops. Increase attributes such as Capacitor and EHP by approximately 5%.
 
Skills
Mothership Command Technology. Its skill category has been changed from Cooperative Combat to Fleet Support.
All Capital Ship Engineering skills. Changed its Warp Capacitor Consumption from -30%/-30%/-30% to -40%/-50%/-60%.
Advanced Capital Ship Components Production Optimization skills are now available. They can reduce the ISK cost of producing different capital ship components (which can be purchased directly with CONCORD Credit or in limited quantities in the recommended Store)
 
Ore Compression
All stations have discontinued the service of compressing common ores into high-density polymerized ores, replacing them with rich ore compression lines, significantly reducing its Fuel Usage, and adding the compression functionality for Moon Ore, as follows:
After the adjustment, common ores like Spodumain will be compressed into Rich Spodumain or their corresponding rich ores. Compared to before, the fuel cost has now dropped by approximately 60% when compressing the same amount of ores.
Reduce C70's fuel cost by approximately 80%, and reduce C50 and C6's fuel cost by over 90%.
Moon Ores such as Stellarite can now be compressed into rich ores using the new compression production line in the station. The corresponding uncompressed ores can be obtained by reprocessing them (they will not be reprocessed into materials).
 
Construction
When Manufacturing Fullero-Ferrocene and other intermediate materials, the manufacturing quantity per batch has been increased from a maximum of 10 Groups to 20 Groups.
Capsuleer Scout Outpost. Changed the ratio of Morphite and intermediate materials requirements, reducing the total cost by approximately 8%.
Corporation Scout Outpost. Changed the ratio of Morphite and intermediate materials requirements, reducing the total cost by approximately 7%.
 
Market
To improve the convenience of capital ship trading when there are insufficient transport ships, capital ships can now be delivered to the Trading Center in the Jita System. At the same time, to ensure the orderly operation of the delivery industry, the capital ship delivery fee has been increased.
Capital Ship Delivery Fee = 1,000,000,000 + 100,000,000 x Delivery Distance (Lightyear).
The delivery fee for supercarriers is twice that of regular capital ships.
Please note that the vast majority of capital ships cannot enter High Sec for combat. After purchase, the capital ship must remain packaged and be delivered to another Low Sec Trading Center using the station delivery feature before it can be assembled and deployed.
 
Structure
Capsuleer Outpost. The fuel cap has been reduced to the same level as the Capsuleer Scout Outpost. The unanchoring time has been reduced from 48 Hours to 24 Hours, and the Minimum Hull Reinforcement Duration has been reduced from 48 Hours to 24 Hours.
Capsuleer Scout Outpost now comes with Cloning Center service by default. Unanchoring time was reduced from 36 hours to 24 hours. TIPS will now display Armor Reinforcement Duration (Note: It lasts only 1 hour, different from standard models)
Corporation Outpost III: Time to upgrade to Corporation Citadel reduced from 16 days to 7 days (Note: Cannot be downgraded after upgrade)
Temporarily removed the Corporation Frontline Outpost Blueprint from sale on the Jita Market
 
With technological advancements, Corporation Frontline Outpost technology has been applied to Reconnaissance Outposts. In two weeks, Corporation Reconnaissance Outposts and Capsuleer Scout Outposts will be able to anchor in the Etherium Reach region, and Wasteseeker Siege Bases will appear in the Etherium Reach region.
 
Relics Scramble
Reduce the relic's respawn interval by 50%, Loyalty Points gained from single data gathering increased by 67%, event extended to June 30th.
 
Implants
All Implants: Durability cap increased from 100 to 255.
Focused Crystal Stability Reinforcement Unit: Focused State Duration Bonus increased from 2~10 seconds to 3~15 seconds, Focused Energy Dissipation Protection increased from 5%~25% to 6%~30%
Pulse Crystal Flight Time Reinforcement Unit V3-V5: Armor cost reduced from 8%/10%/12% to 6%/9%/12%, and Capacitor Conversion increased from 4%/5%/6% to 4%/6%/8%.
Multifrequency Crystal Overclocker Mastery branch also reduces the activation time of Energy Neutralizers
Thermal Circulation: Railgun activation time penalty upon overheating reduced from +300% to +200%
Kinetic Burst: It will now correctly display the Tracking Speed penalty upon activation (this attribute decreases with level, from -80% to -44%. This is a text-only change, with no change to its actual effect)
Sniping Technology. New bonus: Passive +7.5% to +30% optimal and falloff range.
Warhead Charge. New effect: Each type of warhead can penetrate corresponding resistance by 0% to 3% (increases with level).
Support Projection
Inflight Disruption Warhead: Removed the damage penalty, and the disruption chance has been changed from 20% to 12%-21%.
ECM Warhead: Its damage penalty has been changed from -15% to -14% to -8%.
EMP Warhead: Its damage penalty has been changed from -20% to -11% to - 14% to -8%.
Automatic Defense
Changed the Automatic Defense effect to be effective against drones in outer space by default without selecting the Remote Drone Charge branch.
The Reload Disruption Warhead base interval has been reduced from 60 seconds to 45 seconds.
Logistics Optimization Branch: Activation Time bonus changed from -30 to -15 (synchronized with base interval adjustment). New effect: Drone EHP +7.5% to 30%, Drone signature radius -7.5% to -30%.
Remote Drone Charge. Effect changed to: drone damage +1.5% to 15% and drone velocity +6% to 24%.
 
Reactive Implant
Ship Armor and Structure Adjustment unit. Changed the structure penalty from -10% to -7.5%.
Sensor Dampening Resistance unit. Changed the Scan Resolution penalty from -10% to -7.5%.
Targeting Disruption Resistance unit. Changed the Turret Tracking Speed penalty from -10% to -7.5%..
Target Painter Resistance unit. Changed the Signature Radius from 10% to 7.5%.
Guidance Disruption Resistance unit. Changed the penalty to missile flight time from -10% to -7.5%.
Capacitor Warfare Resistance unit. Changed the emergency capacitor recharge effect from -10% to -7.5%.
 
New Content
Thunderstrike - Disruptive Lance
The Amarr Empire has released a more advanced Annihilator Doomsday Weapon, which is more destructive and releases arcs that neutralize the target ship's capacitor.
This model can cause capacitor neutralization effects while ignoring part of the target's Capacitor Warfare Resistance, and the beam strength and beam radius have also been partially improved.
How to obtain: Obtain through Industrial Manufacturing or Store for a limited time.
 
New Arrivals
The Recommended Store now has a new arrival: the Thunderstrike Lance Supply, which can be opened to receive the new Thunderstrike Lance and plenty of Ascension Neural Compilers (Clone Bound).
 
Navigator Program
Event Duration: March 12, 2025, 08:00 - March 31, 2025, 08:00 (UTC+0)
The Navigator Program is now here again! Top up a total of 499 AUR to start a new round of monthly Skill Point Supplies. Top up to a certain amount of AUR for additional rewards. Please refer to the in-game descriptions for details.
 
Bug Fixes

Fixed the issue where the icon of the supply box did not turn gray after being looted.
