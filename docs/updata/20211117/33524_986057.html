Patch Notes - November 17

2021-11-17

We will undergo maintenance on 11/17 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game.


November 17 Version Patch Notes
New Event
Interstellar Bazaar: Neonrain
Event Duration: After maintenance on November 17, 2021 - 08:00 (UTC+0) on December 1
The Interstellar Bazaar is a celebration for people across the whole of New Eden. This year's theme is "Neonrain". There is brand new dance music and dazzling station decor to add to the festivities. Obtain and open Lucky Chests to gain Luck. Have fun!


New Nanocore
Neonrain Nanocore
The Interstellar Bazaar-exclusive "Neonrain" nanocore is the most dazzling nanocore to date. It can provide a great comprehensive attribute bonus while decorating your ship with eye-catching musical visualizations. Obtain and fit this nanocore to your ship and explore New Eden to the beat of the music.


Lucky Chest
Lucky Chests have arrived at the New Eden Store, which contain the brand new Neonrain nanocore. Open Lucky Chests for a chance of winning the lucky prize of the day. You can also use discount codes in the event for even greater value. In addition, you can get 3 Daily Lucky Chests every day during the event by logging in.
Special Note: Most of the daily rewards for Lucky Chests come from the additional insurance purchases from the previous Neon Carnival event.


Lucky Leaderboard
Open Lucky Chests in New Eden Store or daily mails to get Luck. The total amount of Luck you gained each day will be ranked on the leaderboard.
Daily Reward Rules:
Rank 1-20: Neonrain Nanocore Opt Box (Capital Ship)
Rank 21-200: Neonrain Nanocore Random Box (Non-Capital Ship) 


Optimizations
1. Updated estimated prices based on successful transactions in the Market.
2. Improved: the creation and acceptance process of contracts.
3. Enabling Showdown Mode on a dreadnought immediately removes its undock protection.
4. Updated the design for the Back button on the bottom left on the Ship Tree screen.
5. Unprocessed requests pending approval by the corporation will be displayed on top.
6. A new login screen has been added for the upcoming "Interstellar Bazaar".
