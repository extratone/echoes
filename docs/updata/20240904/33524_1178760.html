Patch Notes 0904

2024-09-04

To New Eden Capsuleers, 
    We will apply a hotfix update on 2024/9/4 at 08:00 (UTC+0). Capsuleers can continue playing as normal without any disruptions. 

Relics Scramble Registration Open
Registration Start Time: September 4, 2024, 08:00:00 (UTC+0)
With the upgrade of scanning technology, CONCORD's intelligence division has discovered Sleeper Relics in certain Systems, which were previously hidden stashes used by Sleepers to store items collected from known space. The four major Empires have formed Militias to explore these Relics. With CONCORD's coordination, the Empires are allowing Capsuleers from New Eden to sign up and join their respective Faction Militias for the first time ever.
Capsuleers will join the faction's Militia as an individuals. After joining, they may be attacked by other Militia's members in systems at war, and CONCORD will not prevent such combat. This will disregard Capsuleer's own Corporation friendly/hostile states.
Once each faction recruits a certain number of Capsuleers, CONCORD will limit the number ratio of each faction. After leaving the militia, you will need to wait one day before rejoining. Interested Capsuleers are advised to join the militia as soon as possible.

Combat Start Time: September 11, 2024, 08:00:00 (UTC+0)
After the registration phase ends, CONCORD will make the locations of these relics public. The location information will be available no matter you join the militia or not.
Capsuleers can open the Star Charts to check which systems are at war. Upon entering a system, they can directly find large relics or discover smaller, more hidden relics through scanning. Within the relics, they can collect relic data or ores, and defeat Sleepers or other Militia's members to obtain loot, merits, or LP.
If a Capsuleer joins the Militia and is destroyed by other Militia's members in a war system, the faction insurance companies will provide bonus compensation for the Insurance Points actually spent on such losses. The current bonus compensation rates are: 75% for Regular Ship and 25% for Capital Ship
You can still leave and join the Militia after combat starts, but all Ranks and Merits will be reset.

Engine Update Notice
Engine version 5.2.2 and above do not support 32-bit Android operating systems. If the game can no longer run after installation, please choose another device.

Fixed Issues (Supplementary Announcement)
Fixed the issue of delayed updates in the local member list after passing through a Stargate.
Fixed the issue where purchasing Unit Lucky Chests did not grant Region Development attempts, and the missing attempts have been made up.
Fixed the issue where the damage penalty of repair modules and rapid-fire modules was not showing (it actually has a -20% penalty similar to the mode).
Fixed the issue where the Fighter Damage Bonus under the primary attribute was ineffective on Light Fighters.
Fixed the issue where defeating NPCs might deselect the target.
