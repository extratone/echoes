In order to make sure that everyone has a fair opportunity to apply for this Content Creators program, we are now making an official criteria for all the applicants who are willing to become an EVE Echoes Content Creator.

Please note that only the applicant meeting all the criteria for the category he or she is applying for would be considered. Don't worry if you don't meet the minimums, these numbers are designed to the aspirational but reasonably attainable! So, if you're just starting out, you can work your way up with steady effort in due time.

All content created by our Content Creators should adhere to the EVE Echoes Content Creators Guidelines. Content Creators who repeatedly violate these guidelines would be removed from the Content Creators Program.

Existing Content Creators who fail to meet minimum requirements will be given one additional review period to try and meet the requirements again in order to remain in the Program before being removed. If removed, an applicant must meet minimum requirements for at least 1 month to be eligible again.

Final admittance to the Content Creators Program is at the discretion of Netease. Minimum requirements may be changed at any time and are not a guarantee of acceptance.

Please note your application will be reviewed on the second and fourth Friday of each month. Due to the amount of applications, we can only reply the applicants who successfully get the role. And an email will be sent out by the next Monday of the reviewing day.

EVE ECHOES CONTENT GUIDELINES

EVE Echoes Content Creators are expected to follow just a few basic guidelines.

Content from EVE Echoes Content Creators is expected to:

Maintain a friendly and welcoming atmosphere. Even if you do not necessarily like a certain playstyle or group, keep any chiding friendly and playful.

In the case of contests or giveaways for in-game rewards provided by Netease, you should provide all viewers with an equal chance to win. Such giveaways should not be restricted only to subscribers or patrons. For more details, please read the official EVE Echoes Content Creation Terms of Use.

Avoid behavior which could be construed as racist, bigot, harassment or otherwise exclusionary to any persons in or out of the game.

Avoid negative or disruptive behavior towards other content creators.

Try to keep discussion of EVE features or development constructive (you don’t have to pretend you love everything about the game)

Try to avoid your content being predominantly negative. We do hope you’re having some fun too!

Do not promote any violation of EVE Echoes Content Creation Terms of Use.

Do not contain or promote illegal, racist, sexist, hateful, pornographic or other offensive material.
