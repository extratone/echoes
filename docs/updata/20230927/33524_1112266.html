Patch Notes 0927

2023-09-27

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 9/27/2023 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

New Event
Login Rewards
Open Time: 08:00 October 1, 2023 - 08:00 October 8, 2023 (UTC+0)
Log in for 7 days during the event to claim generous rewards.
Meanwhile, CONCORD has distributed the mysterious boxes collected from Cobalt Edge to capsuleers en-masse. Open them for a chance to get the valuable treasures of the ancient scavengers.

New Arrivals
Available Time: 08:00 October 1, 2023 - 08:00 October 11, 2023 (UTC+0)
The Limited-time Growth Bundle (Tesseract Star Core, Nanocore Materials, and more) is now available in the Store to help Capsuleers explore New Eden.
Once the event has started, Capsuleers can check and purchase the bundle in Store -> Bestseller Combo -> Hot Selection.

Optimizations:
Valuation updated based on the market transaction record.
The AI core will no longer activate modules such as Damage Controls and Scanners.

Bug Fixes:
Fixed an issue where the number of products in the Reverse Engineering interface might show as 0.
Fixed an issue where the Anchor Capsuleer Outpost interface might show Corporation Outpost Tech Bonus.
