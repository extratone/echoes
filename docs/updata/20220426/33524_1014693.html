New Content
Capital Ship Nanocore
Raata Sunset Series Blue Nanocore now available in New Eden!
This series of nanocores covers four Carriers and four Dreadnoughts of the four Empires and can be obtained by Reverse Engineering. Its reverse material can be obtained in Nihilus Space Lv.9 and Lv.10.

Corporation Mission
The Corporation system will undergo a major update. By authorizing the following key tools, Corporation collaboration will be greatly improved.

Corporation Mission System
Corporation missions are designed to create a bridge between mission initiators and recipients.
With this system, Corporation Management can check members in, distribute ships and rewards, assemble fleets, and publish announcements. Corporation members can also initiate compensation for damages, ask for supplies, submit supplies, and order ships.
In addition to regular templates, there are many more custom modules to be discovered. You can bring up any demand and seek response from the Corporation.

Corporation Points System
Corporation Points are designed to provide an efficient monetization system to motivate members.
Members can gain points issued by the Corp by completing missions published by Corporation Management, or through Corporation Market trading. The total amount of each individual's points will be displayed on the Corp Members page.
All the rights for adding and deducting points are controlled by the Corporation Management. Therefore, the use of the points, such as their exchange value, relies on the Corp's guarantee. You need to understand the Corp points system and make sure that it can be trusted.

Corporation Market System
The Corporation Market is designed to facilitate transactions within the Corp.
In addition to travel to the New Eden Trading Center, this tool allows you to use your own supplies to trade with the Corporation in the Corporation Office. The system provides two transaction methods: Points and ISK. All transaction orders are created by the Corporation Management. In addition to initiating the purchase and sale of materials, the Corporation can also sell ships containing modules.

Corporation Citadel New Features
Corporation Citadel can immediately scan and discover the highest level pirate base in the system by consuming a certain amount of fuel. If a pirate base already exists in the current system, the old pirate base beacon will disappear. This scan function has a 48-hour cooldown.
In addition, the layout of the Corporation Citadel management page has been adjusted so more functions can be added to the Corporation Citadel in the future.

New In Store - Interstellar Secret Trafficking Container
During their exploration of unknown regions, Capsuleers found these encrypted safes, which contain precious items such as Faction Nanocores and Capital Ship Nanocores. You can open them with specific decoding credentials. If they are destroyed, the items inside may also be destroyed.
Capsuleers can obtain the Interstellar Secret Trafficking Containers and decoding credentials in the Verge of Silence event. They will also be available in the New Eden Store for a limited time.
