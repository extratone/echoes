Patch Notes - July 26

2023-07-26

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 7/26/2023 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

NEW EVENT
Navigator Program
Duration: UTC0 2023.7.26 8:00 - 08:00 on 14 August, 2023
The Navigator program is now available again. Recharge 499 AUR to unlock a new round of monthly Skill Points Supply. You will get additional rebates after recharging a certain number of AUR. See the in-game rules for details.
New Premium Navigator Program: After unlocking the Navigator Program, Capsuleers can spend another 499 AUR to unlock the Premium Navigator Program and claim double Skill Points every day

Supercarrier Manufacturing Contest
The first winners of the Supercarrier Manufacturing Contest have been updated! The data is collected from all active supercarrier manufacturing jobs as of July 19.
The event will end after the maintenance on August 2, so please be quick on joining the event, Capsuleers.
The Announcement, Report & Review period will last until the maintenance on August 16. If Capsuleers have any information related to any rule violations, please contact our customer service.
At the end of the review period, all individual rewards (including rank rewards and base rewards) will be issued to the manufacturer, and Corporation rewards will be issued to the Corporation CEO.

Balance adjustment
Nereus Nanocore can now be installed on all Nereus models, including Nereus Combat.
Fleet Commander now receives all group recovery effects from all 5 squads.
Cargo Hold Management Implant now affects Delivery Holds as well.
With the development of technology, it is now possible to compress Tritanium into a form with higher crystal density at outposts.
It is now possible to reprocess the Damaged ORE Capital Hull.
It is now possible to reprocess the Nanocore of Praxis ships.

Optimizations:
Valuation updated based on the market transaction record.
Optimized Ship TIPS, where it is now possible to check the remaining fuel of the ship currently piloting without undocking.
Optimized the categorization of Faction Ships in the Market, making them easier to search for.
Optimized the description for Special Edition Ships, including Can-Yue, where their IPs are no longer increased.
Changed one of the missions in Road to Set Sail from “Join a match of Faction Wargames” to “Earn ISK”.

Bug Fixes:
Fixed an issue where there was a chance that Reverse Power skills could not be activated with Pulse Crystal implants.
Fixed an issue where modules such as Command Burst would play their special effects once after being turned off.
Fixed an issue where special effects of modules such as Burst would continue to play instead of disappearing under rare circumstances.
Fixed an issue where modules such as Cynosural Field Generator would show that they are being deactivated under rare circumstances.
Fixed an issue where the damage transfer attribute was displayed inaccurately in the TIPS of the Supercarrier Cover Module.
Fixed an issue in the fitting window and the Fighter TIPS where the damage of every fighter set was overcounted by one fighter, causing the numbers displayed in TIPS to be higher than the actual values.
Fixed an issue where the unique bonus of Supercarrier would cause an increase in jump power consumption.
Fixed an issue where Defense Disruption Burst was ineffective against some NPCs.
Fixed an issue where the damage bonus of Bombard Tactics would sometimes not be removed after dealing damage.
