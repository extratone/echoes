Patch Notes - March 9

2022-03-09

EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 3/9/2022 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

New Content
New Eden Store Update
Duration: After maintenance on Mar 9, 2022 until Mar 30, 2022
Reopened the Splendid Coupon Store. Coupons can be obtained through Ship-rental Project and in-store purchases. New Wave Graph Packs and Auto Salvagers can be purchased through the Store. Battleship Nanocores, Prayer SKIN Opt Boxes and Classified II Gravitational Wave Graphs are available with a 10% limited-time discount.
After adjustment, the chance of obtaining Classified II Gravitational Wave Graph from Secret Trafficking Container has increased.

Auto Salvage Technology
Available: After the maintenance on Mar 9, 2022
At the request of the Capsuleers, the CONCORD and ORE decided to authorize a license for the use of the Auto Salvage Technology.
The Auto Salvager is a mid-slot module used to salvage pirate wrecks. It can automatically identify and loot pirate wrecks upon activation within a specific range. You can expand this range and loot more wrecks simultaneously by fitting more than one or more advanced Auto Salvagers.

Auto Salvage Technology is a new technology used to increase the Salvaging Range and can only be learned through Skill Chips. Auto Salvagers and Skill Chips can be traded in the market.


How to get Auto Salvagers?
Use "Classified II Gravitational Wave Graph" to explore abnormal Gravitational Fluctuation and get a chance to obtain Auto Salvage Technology Chips (3 types) and Auto Salvagers (3 types).
Basic Auto Salvagers can be exchanged in the Splendid Coupon Store.

Road to Set Sail
Available: After the maintenance on Mar 9, 2022
In order to help novice Capsuleers to improve, the CONCORD has prepared a series of Road to Set Sail missions.
Complete the daily list for daily rewards. Complete all lists for 7 days for the final reward.

Optimizations
1. Updated estimated prices based on successful transactions in the Market.
2. Batch Investigation can now be implemented on multiple Ancient Remains.
3. There is no 24-hour limit for returning Capsuleers to rejoin a Corporation for the first time.
4. Adjusted the Omega reward for Day 7 and Alpha reward for Day 8 in the Pilot Cultivation Program.
5. Optimized the Planetary Mining. It's easier to extend the mining time for multiple planets now.
6. Adjusted the upgrade feature for Cosmic Anomaly. Invisible players won't be counted for the upgrade.
7. Optimized the Destroy feature. When destroying a ship, only the ship and its Rigs will be destroyed while its Cargo, Modules and Drones will be moved to the station hangar.
8. Adjusted the Repackage feature. Ships and Nanocores can't be repackaged if multiple items are selected at a time.
9. Optimized the failed-login prompt.

Bug Fixes
1. Fixed the issue where the filter on Combat Log wasn't displayed.
