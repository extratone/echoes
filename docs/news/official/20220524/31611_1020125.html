Noctis

The Noctis marks ORE's entry into the lucrative bulk salvaging market. Building on their successes integrating Marauder-class tractor technology into the Orca command platform and innovations in automated salvaging technology, they designed a compact, affordable wreck recovery solution.

Skil: Expert Industrial Ship Defense Upgrade, Command

Stats: 2.5-4.5k (Shield), 2.5k (Armor), 4-5k (Structure), 0.5k (Powergrid), 2-2.5k (Capacitor), 500k-525k (Recharge)
