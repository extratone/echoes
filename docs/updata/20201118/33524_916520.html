Patch Notes - Nov. 18

2020-11-18

We will be having our weekly maintenance on Nov 18 at 08:00 UTC. During this time, players won't be able to log into the game. This is expected to last for two hours.

Here is a list of specific changes that will be applied after maintenance ends.

 

New Features:

1. The number of reward items and receiving status display are increased for Advanced Missions.

2. The prestige display rules of drone have been adjusted.

3. Bounty quests were added in CONCORD for Advanced Pilot Tutorials.

 

Fixes:

1. Fixed the display error of the activation time of the Omega activation email.

2. Modified the wrong volume of some transport tasks.

3. Modified the Bounty Rewards for some News Missions errors.

4. Corrected the wrong Elite enemies in the mission of the Quafe Legion.
