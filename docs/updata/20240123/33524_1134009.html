Patch Notes 0124

2024-01-23

Dear pilots of New Eden,

EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 1/24/2024 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

 

Balance Announcement

Inquisitor and Scout Cosmic Anomaly

The natural withdrawal time for fleets in the Cosmic Anomaly has been reduced from 10 days to 1 day. If not attacked, they will withdraw with all their materials after 1 day.

Note: The current natural withdrawal time for other special cosmic anomalies is 1-3 days. The higher the difficulty, the longer the withdrawal time

 

New Arrivals

Available Time: 08:00 January 24, 2024 - 08:00 March 13, 2024 (UTC+0)

Wasteseeker I Gravitational Wave Graph

With a deeper understanding of the Wasteseekers, CONCORD scanned the systems and discovered many locations suspected to be important Wasteseeker supply storage points. CONCORD is now offering Wasteseeker I Gravitational Wave Graph for capsuleers to explore these locations.

Wasteseeker I Gravitational Wave Graph records the supply storage points of the Wasteseeker Drones, and these locations have been found throughout New Eden.

Unlike the Classified series Gravitational Wave Graph, these locations are guarded by Wasteseeker Drones, and the supply boxes mainly contain drones and Wasteseeker-related items.

Furthermore, there is a chance to obtain the Wasteseeker II Gravitational Wave Graph from Wasteseeker I Gravitational Wave Graph’s loot crates. The Wasteseeker II Graph contains information on even more important Wasteseeker Capital Ship's hidden supply storage points, which are often guarded by a few capital ships. It is recommended to prepare for combat against capital ships beforehand.

 

Skill Points and Insurance Points Discount Pack

Available Time: 08:00 January 24, 2024 - 08:00 March 13, 2024 (UTC+0)

To raise the skill level of capsuleers and strengthen their combat ability against powerful foes like the Wasteseekers, the Skill Points and Insurance Points discount packs are now available on the New Eden Store. Please buy them as you see fit, capsuleers.

During the event, if you purchase the event pack in a single purchase up to the specified quantity, you will be gifted an “Specialization Skill Opt Box”.

Hint: You can check the purchase quantity required for the gift on the purchase page. Once obtained, the gift will be distributed via mail.

In appreciation for the long-term support of the capsuleers, the Weapon Skill Opt Box (Clone Bound) is now available at the Store once more. Capsuleers may purchase it with ISK, limited to 1 purchase during the event.

 

Blueprints Rerun at the CONCORD Pass Shop

The Swarm General Fire-control System Blueprint and Signal Amplifier Blueprint are limited-time items, which are now available once more until March 13.

 

Optimizations

Valuation updated based on the market transaction record.

 

Bug Fixes

Fixed an issue where the activated Expanded Missile Storage node of the Barrage Repression Implant may become invalid after jumping through a Stargate.

Fixed an issue where NPCs using Warp Disruptor may not turn off their modules after becoming out the range.

Fixed the issue where there were duplicate ship nanocores in the CyberX Opt Box (Regular).
