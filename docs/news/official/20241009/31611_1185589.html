EVE Galaxy Conquest is launching globally on Oct 29

2024-10-09

From the creators of acclaimed MMO EVE Online, prepare to enter the vast cosmos of New Eden where intense factional warfare and diplomacy will force you to consider every decision, find allies, and outsmart your foes in a battle for seasonal supremacy.

You will start in an uncharted corner of the star sector, exploring your surroundings to secure your foothold in space. Exploiting nearby resources will allow you to upgrade your space station and ships for more ambitious ventures. As you continually expand and secure your territory you will encounter other players. Will you choose friendship? Or extermination? We leave that choice to you.

It is our honor to announce that our new friend of EVE Universe, EVE Galaxy Conquest is now launching globally on October 29!  
Diving into an epic universe, exploiting unknown resources, and conquering your ambitious ventures!

For more info: https://www.evegalaxyconquest.com/
