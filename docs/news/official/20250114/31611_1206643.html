Soaring Dragon Nanocore

The dragon's soar stirs the mountains, as its mighty thunder ushers in the spring for all! The new "Soaring Dragon" Nanocore is about to make a powerful debut in New Eden!

Event Time: January 16, 2025 08:00 ~ February 27, 2025 08:00

The new "Soaring Dragon" Nanocore draws inspiration from Chinese dragons and holographic projection technology, simulating a holographic projection of a dragon coiling around the ship's hull that symbolize the dragon's protection as Capsuleers explore the vast galaxy. Unlike previous ship SKINs that were limited to decoration on its surface, the "Soaring Dragon" aims to provide Capsuleers with a vivid and dynamic visual experience.

Additionally, the appearance of the "Soaring Dragon" core features effects of wind and thunder, as if a dragon is weaving through clouds with thunderous roars heralding the vibrant life of the coming spring.

The "Soaring Dragon" core supports a wide range of color customization options, allowing Capsuleers to personalize the ship to their likings.

Wasteseeker Combat Test

Event Time: January 16, 2025 08:00 ~ February 27, 2025 08:00

CONCORD has successfully extracted data from Wasteseeker wrecks in Cobalt Edge and recreated it in virtual space. By having Capsuleers engage in battles with virtual Wasteseekers in the virtual space, CONCORD can observe and record Wasteseeker behavior and Capsuleers can improve their combat skills. Being defeated in this combat will not result in ship damage or battle reports.

During the event, Capsuleers can enter the Combat Test through the "Faction Wargames" button in Station Services.

The Wasteseeker Combat Test is a no-loss combat mode, with three difficulties: Expert, Super Expert, and Capital Super Expert. Capsuleers must complete the challenges in sequence. Rewards for challenge missions are issued once, and repeating the same challenge will not yield bonus rewards.

Expert & Super Expert: Suitable for Advanced Battleship tonnage or higher, with enemies mainly being medium and small high-speed Wasteseeker ships.

Capital Super Expert: Suitable for ships of Capital Ship tonnage or higher. Besides small, high-speed ships, enemies will gradually include high-damage, high-defense large-tonnage ships, including carriers equipped with high-speed fighters.

Event Rewards: LP, Spring Dragonscale Currency, Exclusive Medal, Personal Merit

Allegiance

An experimental Destroyer launched by CONCORD DED, designed to search for Pirate Bases in dangerous areas and deliver powerful strikes against Pirate Facilities.

The Allegiance adheres to CONCORD's consistent design philosophy, boasting strong defense capabilities and powerful fire control radar. It prioritizes agility and the Warp Drive over flight velocity, allowing flexible use of various Small Turrets and Missiles.

On a tactical level, the Allegiance can be fitted with a Covert Ops Cloaking Device, Covert Cynosural Field Generator, Missile Launcher Bomb, Large Torpedo Launcher, and Bombard Tactics modules, allowing it to effectively strike large enemy targets.

Based on various technological achievements during the development of the Allegiance, the DED has simultaneously launched 3 types of fighters or lightweight ships.

The "Guard" Light Attack Fighter is a new type of fighter incorporating various Allegiance technologies. It emphasizes agility over flight velocity, with high weapon accuracy, fast rate of fire, and low damage. With the aid of new fire control radar, it can flexibly handle multiple small enemies, making it suitable for defending the mothership or providing long-range fire support.

The "Iron Guard" Heavy Attack Fighter is the heavy model used by Supercarriers, with high defense, good agility, high weapon accuracy, and fast rate of fire, suitable for defending the mothership or providing long-range fire support against enemy capital ships.

"The Lightweight Allegiance Destroyer, after undergoing lightweight modifications, has its tactical module and large devices such as the warp drive removed. Despite these changes, it maintains high defense capabilities and agility, allowing it to flexibly engage medium to close-range enemy targets.
