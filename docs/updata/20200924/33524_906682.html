Patch Notes-Sep. 24

2020-09-24

Capsuleers:

We will be having our weekly maintenance on Sep 24 at 08:00 UTC. During this time, players won't be able to log into the game. This is expected to last for two hours.

Here is a list of specific changes that will be applied after maintenance ends.

 

  • Since the base mechanics in the High-Sec areas were widely exploited, the chance for players to see the Scout and Inquisitor has now been reduced for Level 1-3 bases.
  • Improved the game client's rendering efficiency.
  • Updated estimated prices in the Market.
  • While on autopilot, busy systems will be avoided.
  • The Trade Lock won't be triggered for account contracts which are under the same account.
  • Sliding function is now supported when switching tabs on the Overview list.
  • Now if the player loses connection in a Cosmic Anomaly, ship will try to warp out.
  • Added: the entry "4.8.7 The First Disruption on Wormholes" to the CONCORD's Guide to New Eden. Specifically, players who experienced the maintenance on August 26 will receive a commemorative stamp for their entry.
  • Improved AI pirate's target-switching efficiency.
  • Added a limit to the number of module special effects that can be shown in the same scene.
  • Guest accounts are now banned from transactions. They will need to link their accounts before using the feature.
  • Added a commemorative stamp related to the server breakdown on August 26.
  • Added warning confirmation for locking allies.
  • Guest accounts need to carry out account linking before they can transfer supplies.
  • Players will receive a confirmation when their autopilot route includes low-sec or dangerous system in the navigation.
  • Added notification for when player's Resource Extraction container is stolen.
  • Changes have been made to some entries in CONCORD's Guide to New Eden.
  • Added stages 8 and 9 to Advanced Missions.
  • Added an option on the Fleet page to enable/disable equal bounty distribution among fleet members.
  • Added a feature for issuing maintenance support rewards.
  • Players' choice of transportation from their previous order will be remembered for their next order.
  • Added a portal for selling PLEX on the Market page.
  • After completing the Pilot Cultivation Program, players can enable monthly cumulative log-in rewards.
  • Changed the name of the log-in reward to Pilot Cultivation Program. Also, the process for claiming the reward (game client) has been simplified.
  • Added notification to let players know when they forget to load their shipping containers.
  • Changed the button order on the player business card, which can be accessed by tapping player avatars in the contact list or private chat.
  • The Combat Log page now contains more content.
  • Player can change their name and avatar.
  • Corporation are allowed to change logo, name and Corporation Ticker.
  • Market order display has been adjusted from 30 days to 7 days.
  • Estimated price will display in the Market list.
