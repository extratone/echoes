Patch Notes 1204

2024-12-04

To New Eden Capsuleers, 
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 12/4/2024 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance. 

Balance Adjustments
Characters who have joined the Corporation for less than 30 days cannot use the Corporation Market and Corporation Missions.
All stations can now trade flagships (note that most Capital Ships cannot enter Highsec, Capsuleers can transport or deliver them to other stations for assembly)
In Relics Scramble, the war status of a system will persist and will no longer end with the despawn of relics.
Reduced the number of electronic warfare modules fitted by Wasteseeker enemies in Relics Scramble.
After joining the Militia, the merit loss due to death is reduced. The lower the rank, the less the loss.
When structures like Cynosural Jammer Tower are attacked and the Armor Hardening state is activated, their enhanced/inefficient attributes will be used (it's currently considered as normal state, and this change fixes the bug).
Yan-Jung Capital Ship Armor System Refactoring. Shield penalty reduced from -50% to -40%, bonus to armor changed from final value +150% to base value +150% (no longer effective on Armor Plates).

Redeem Starbreak Key Duration Extended
The Redeem Starbreak Key Exchange Store will extend its open time by 1 week. Capsuleers are advised to redeem the remaining keys as soon as possible. After the store closes, redemption will no longer be possible.

Optimizations
All Industrial Ships, freighters, shuttles, and Special Edition Ships will now be displayed in the Ship Tree.
Module with Metalevel 9-10 will now display a new corner mark color.
When a player applies to join a fleet, a red dot alert will appear on the relevant interface.
Added a new battle setting to enable or disable the display of damage numbers taken by yourself.
Optimized the Inventory, SHIPS, and module interfaces to make decimal displays more concise.
It is now required to input the Confirm button when deleting a Supply Cache, reducing the chances of accidental deletion of valuable items.
Upgrade for Starbreak Series AI Core is now available.

New Eden Store Update Hint
The Loyalty Point store will close soon. Some popular items will be moved to the Militia Supply Store, and all Test modules will be temporarily removed. Capsuleers should purchase the any desired items as soon as possible.
A new Exchange Store using multiple currencies will open soon, allowing the exchange of Shuttle with Scholar Points again.

Bug Fixes
Fixed the issue with the hull shield and combat logs icons display for ships like the Marshal.
Fixed the issue where modules might not be displayed after the citadel is downgraded.
Fixed the issue where the Supercarrier Cover Module could stack, causing regular ships to take no damage.
Fixed the issue where the Mailing Lists might not scroll.
Fixed an extremely rare issue where the protocol library attributes of the AI core could be selected twice. Some abnormal data will be replaced with random attributes from the protocol library in the future.
Fixed an issue where Starbreak Reforge might simultaneously increase the max limit of both Shield/Armor.
