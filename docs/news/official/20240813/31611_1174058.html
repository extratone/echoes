EVE Echoes Anniversary Ceremony 2024 Q&A

2024-08-13

Gather in the milky way, set sail together!

EVE Echoes Anniversary ceremony events are about to begin!


Q: When does anniversary ceremony 2024 start? What new content does it include?

Week 1:

1. Start the 7-day time-limited login rewards.

2. Start a 4-week reward event with reduced trading tax rates, increased planet mining output, and increased loot from defeating enemies.

3. Start the Wasteseeker event, where defeating the Wasteseekers can reward various derivative module, drone components, and rare ship blueprint (Shrike, Red-tailed Hawk).

4. Open registration stage for the Voyage Ceremony 2024 and announce the list of successfully registered cooperations.

5. Distribute anniversary blessing emails and rewards.

6. Permanently activate the insurance order bonus event, where both free insurance points and paid insurance points can generate market orders, increasing the market demand for ships and equipment.

7. Permanently reduce the prices of all items in the ISK exchange store except for the Amethyst Cone.

 

Week 2:

1. Start the event of 'Verge of Silence' and the event points store.

2. Start the event of 'Ego Traverse'.

3. Open the anniversary store, including the new ‘Celebration' series nanocores and various special price development items. The core features are bonuses for shield&armor damage and defense bonuses for major faction, with no special mode.

4. Start the event called 'Regional Development’, where purchasing any item has a chance to receive various prizes including implant opt box and new 'Celebration‘ series nanocores.

5. Activate the formation editing function, and our customer service will communicate with corporation CEO about matters related to the Voyage Ceremony.

 

Week 3:

1. Open moon drilling construction and related technologies; at the same time, there will be some adjustments to Corporation Tech, planning to open to level 5.

2. Launch new modules.

3. Launch new types of ships (destroyers and faction battleships).

4. Initiate ongoing balance adjustments to existing ships, modules, implants, etc.

5. Obtain corporation formation, motto, and participant data, and conduct data verification.

 

Week 4:

1. Open the first faction war event gameplay (long-term), through this gameplay, you can improve your rank and gain loyalty points. The rank is the same as the rank in the simulated battle, but can be upgraded to a higher level through faction wars. Subsequent optimizations will continue to be made to this gameplay and more faction war series gameplay will be introduced.

2. Update the loyalty points store, add new modules and blueprints, and remove unpopular items.

3. Hold a voyage ceremony and distribute server-wide rewards.

Q: Why is the opening time of this year's anniversary ceremony later than before?
 

Under normal circumstances, the start time of the anniversary celebration event is around August 10th. This year, due to adjustments in the development plan, we have canceled a play system under development that combines research and development with various functional components. This has affected other gameplay and activities to some extent, so the start time will be slightly delayed. As a result, we will be providing server-wide compensation and benefits.

Expected distribution: 1 million Skill Points, 10 million Lazarus Units, 100 Ascension Neural Compilers, 100 nanites, 100 Amethyst Cones.

Q: What will happen if there are problems during the Voyage Ceremony event?
 

First of all, Concord sincerely thank the pilots for your active participation and steadfast support in this voyage ceremony. The system of the voyage ceremony is very complex, and the preparatory team has conducted comprehensive preliminary tests to ensure the smooth progress of the event and to try to avoid any unexpected situations.

However, experienced clones know that any carefully planned event may face unforeseen challenges. We solemnly promise that if unforeseen circumstances arise that require the event to be suspended, the preparatory team will take immediate action: first, the pilots' data will be restored; second, the exciting moments of the event will be made into a video and uploaded to YouTube for everyone to review; third, all registration fees for participating corporations will be fully refunded. At the same time, high compensation rewards will be given to members of corporations who are unable to participate in the event due to unexpected in-game situations, to express sincere apologies. In addition, compensation will also be prepared for all pilots.

If the voyage ceremony can be successfully held, the joy of this success will also be shared with all pilots on the server, and additional rewards will be prepared for all pilots on the server to thank everyone for their enthusiastic participation and contribution to the gaming community.

Once again, thank you for the understanding and support of all pilots. The preparatory team will continue to work tirelessly to bring you a better gaming experience.
