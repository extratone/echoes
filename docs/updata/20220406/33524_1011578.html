Patch Notes - April 6

2022-04-06

EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 4/6/2022 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

CONCORD Pass Season 8
Pass Duration: Apr. 08, 2022 to May 20, 2022
Season 8 of CONCORD Pass will start on Apr. 08, 2022. A Faction Battleship Nanocore "Clear Sky" is introduced in this update. Insurance points in some CONCORD Pass rewards will be changed to Lazarus Unit. Four new types of CONCORD Pass Missions will also be available. Please check the Rules Details on the CONCORD Pass page for more information.

Inscription of the Galaxy
Please note that we will soon begin to collect information on customized rewards for last season's winning Corporations.

Optimizations
1. Added the "Report" function to the Quick Chat Command.
2. Added "Automatic sharing of Combat Log to the Fleet Channel" in Settings.
3. Added "Fleet Orbiting", "Approach Me" and "Orbit Me" in the Fleet Command.
4. Tapping on a contact in the contact list now redirects you to the private chat with the contact.
5. Some mid-slot modules that are currently unavailable in the market have been hidden.
6. Added a Hide UI feature. Players can find it by tapping the gear button to the right of chat messages.
7. The basic salvaging range of all Salvagers has been increased by 2.5 kilometers.
8. Improved the calculation method of capacitor stability on the Fitting page. The results are now more accurate.

Bug Fixes
1. Fixed the issue where the control range of the Fighter Rigs wasn't displayed.
2. Fixed an issue where Cosmic Anomaly names are sometimes incorrectly displayed.
3. Fixed the issue where the change language page was sometimes inaccessible.
