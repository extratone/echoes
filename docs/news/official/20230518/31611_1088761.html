"CyanSea Cup" Corporation Clash Tournament Coming Soon!

2023-05-18

Get ready for the upcoming "CyanSea Cup" Corporation Clash Tournament in EVE ECHOES! Lead your corporation to battle for glory, and win the grand prize  "CyanSea Carrier", and many other amazing rewards! Check out the tournament details below.

Tournament Schedule
Registration: 0:00 AM UTC, May 18 - 11:59 PM UTC, May 23
Round of 16: May 27 - May 28
Quarter-finals: June 3 - June 4
Semi-finals: June 10
Finals: June 12
*The schedule may be adjusted based on the actual registration situation.
Registration Link: https://forms.gle/ugQ3a39nJz9JYB3y9

Tournament Rules
1. Participation Qualification
1) A maximum of 30 members of each corporation can compete in this tournament. The corporation must be established before May 18. Priority for participation will be given to players who have previously hit on the Inscription of the Galaxy leaderboards. Any remaining qualifications will be determined through a random drawing. Concord will announce the final list of selected participants after the registration phase.
2) The tournament adopts a single elimination system, and the registered Corps will be matched by a random drawing.
3) After the registration, each corporation will select one representative to join the "Arbitration Committee." For any disputes that arise during the match, the remaining representatives from the non-participating corporations will make a judgment by voting and decision-making.

2. Match Mode: Clashes Without Loss
1) Each match is limited to 60 mins. If the time limit is exceeded, the match will end automatically and the winner will be determined based on each side's battle score.
2) The challenge mode needs to be initiated by the corporation with authority and accepted by the other corp. The CEO needs to negotiate the match time with the opponent at least 24 hours before each matches.
3) At the agreed time, players from both sides can enter the battlefield by piloting their ships from any station, Personal Citadel, or Corporation Citadel.
4) In the Clashes Without Loss, players can re-enter the match after their ship has been destroyed multiple times. However, be careful as there will be a cooldown time for entering the battlefield, which increases as the match progresses, with a maximum wait time of 300s.

3. No limitations on ship levels.

4. Game Watching Guidelines
1) 16 to 8 (May 27/28): 8 corporations compete each day, with 4 matches per day (No Livestream and the corporations will arrange the match time by themselves).
2) Quarterfinals (June 3/4): 4 corporations compete each day, with 2 matches per day (No Livestream and the corporations will arrange the match time by themselves).
3) Semifinals and Finals (June 10/12):
4 corporations competes in 2 matches on June 10. The first round of matches will take place from 8:00 AM to 9:00 AM (UTC), and the second round from 9:15 AM to 10:15 AM (UTC).  The third day is for the third-place match and final. The match for third place will take place from 8:00 AM to 9:00 AM (UTC), while the final will be held from 9:15 AM to 10:15 AM (UTC). 
*The schedule may be adjusted based on the actual registration situation.
