Patch Notes 0823

2023-08-23

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 8/23/2023 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

Balance Adjustments
Unstable Wormhole
Increased the collapse speed of unstable wormholes, which will now collapse in about 3 days and randomly spawn again after a while.
Small unstable wormholes may now spawn in Cobalt Edge and low-sec space of the empires. Only battleships and ships with lower tonnage may traverse through them.
These wormholes spawn in great numbers and take about a day to collapse. Due to their weak signal, they can only be discovered via scanning and cannot be directly checked on the starmap.

Structure
Increased the Maximum Anchor Amount of Structures of the Same Type of all Add-on Structures from 5 to 9.

Ship
All Navy Issue Frigates get +1 Low Slots and +8 Powergrid.
Rifter Fleet Issue gets +15 Powergrid, and its Cannon Damage Bonus is increased from 30% to 35%.
Kestrel Naval Issue gets +10 Powergrid, and its Missile Damage is increased from 30% to 35%.

Preview: We will release several balancing updates in September to enhance your experience, and you will receive a gift of up to 10,000,000 Lazarus Units along with them. Please plan your use of Lazarus Units wisely, Capsuleer

New Event
CONCORD Pass
Pass Start Time: UTC +0 August 25, 2023 00:00:00 - October 6, 2023 00:00:00
CONCORD Pass Season 20 will update on August 25, 2023. The latest CONCORD Pass update will bring new content for all:
The Exile series Nanocores are available once more: Special Frigate Trailblazer Nanocore Opt Supply is available for exchange now.

Optimizations:
Valuation updated based on the market transaction record.

Bug Fixes:
Fixed the issue where Navy Issue Frigates had no trait description.
Fixed the issue where the Asteroid Belt would not naturally despawn when it has low ore reserve.
Fixed the issue where it may be possible to see the local player list in Cobalt Edge.
Fixed the issue where the manufacturing product of Capital Remote Armor Repairer Rig Blueprint was incorrect.
The improperly priced Complier Supply and Nanocore Material Box have been removed from the Store. Capsuleers who have purchased them will be compensated.
We have fixed some issues with the Voyage Ceremony. Due to the fact that some players are unable to participate in the event and that redoing the event requires a lengthy preparation time, CONCORD has decided not to reopen the Voyage Ceremony on August 27th. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will be providing compensation to all players soon, as well as refunding the sign-up fees for all participating corps.
