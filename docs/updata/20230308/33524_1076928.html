Patch Notes - MAR 08

2023-03-08

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 3/8/2023 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

NEW EVENT
Daily Special
Introducing the Daily Discount Gift pack. The points earned from purchasing the pack can be exchanged for goodies such as Nanocores in the store.

Growth Fund Update
To reward Capsuleers for their unremitting efforts in upgrading Tech Level, CONCORD is offering generous rewards to the Growth Fund. Unlock the Growth Fund to get rare items such as Dark Halo Battleship Core,
Compressed Nanites, etc.
Capsuleers who have already unlocked and claimed the Growth Fund can redeem various newly added rewards on the event page.

Inscription of the Galaxy: Conquest
Duration: Mar. 9, 2023 8:00:00 to May. 19, 2023 8:00:00 (UTC+0)
Explore the stars in the vast universe. The new season, Conquest, is about to kick off. Please view the in-game event description for more details.

CONCORD Pass
Duration: Mar. 10, 2023 0:00:00 to Apr. 21, 2023 0:00:00 (UTC+0)
CONCORD Pass Season 16 will update on March 10, 2023. New content has been added in this update:
Clear Sky Series Faction Nanocores are available for exchange again.
Note: Skill Chips for Module Invention Principles will be removed from CONCORD Pass Shop on May 31, 2023 0:00:00 (UTC+0), and will be available through other means in the future.

New Modules
New Engineering Rigs:
Ship Hold Optimization and Ore Hold Optimization, which can both increase cargo hold for corresponding industrial ships.
Rig Optimizations:
Updated the Cargo Hold Optimization icon.

Optimizations
1. The estimated price has been updated based on the market transaction record.
2. Improved the judgment logic of rigs in the fitting to reduce unnecessary disassembly.
3. Increased the Remaining Fuel for Ansible Stargate to 10.24 million.
4. In Corporation Clash, enemy stealth ships will not count toward the number of enemies displayed on the map.
5. After repackaging Nanocores, all of the returned Computing Chips and the database will be changed to a generic version and will be Clone Bound.

Bug Fixes
1. Fixed the issue where non-integrated integrated rigs could not be removed or destroyed.
2. Fixed the issue where Lightweight IC Frigates could still use Warp Disruption when out of range.
3. Fixed the issue where the target's name might not display in Overview when the network connection is unstable.
4. Fixed the issue with activated modules not showing as activated in the client during large-scale battles.
5. Fixed the issue where the Micro Eject Mode of Voyage Nanocore could not be activated at the same as the Microwarpdrive.
