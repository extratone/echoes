Patch Notes - Nov 16

2022-11-16

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 11/16/2022 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

Balance Adjustments
Ships
Mordu's Legion Ship Flight Velocity increased by 15%.
Angel Ship Flight Velocity increased by 5%.
Raised the Special Cargo Hold Capacity for Amarr and Minmatar industrial ships.
Increased the Fuel Compartment Hold Capacity on all ships by 4 times.
Cruiser II has two new single-attribute resistance bonuses of 15% and 25%, respectively. Types of resistance vary due to the different technologies each Faction is capable of.
Barghest Missile Explosion Velocity raised to 7.5%.
Enhanced versatility on Nestor ships, added Armor Link Module and Logistic Drone Bonus, the total amount of other bonuses remains unchanged, and the bonus skills are re-matched.
Archon Fighter Optimal Range raised to 7.5%.

Ship Modes
Under Sniper Mode, added 25% Scan Resolution, ship velocity penalty decreased to 90%, raised Drone Control Range to 125%.
Originally designed to attack immovable Structures and extremely slow Capital Ships, Siege mode should be a low-precision, high-damage assault. As a result, the Range and Explosion Radius penalties have been reduced, making them more in line with its core features. Adjustments are as follows：
Explosion Radius penalty has been reduced to 100%. New bonus： Missile Velocity reduced by 10%, same with the turret range.

Weapons
Small Rifled Railgun Optimal Range increased by 1km.
Medium Rifled Railgun Optimal Range increased by 2km, Tracking Speed increased by 3.1.
Large Rifled Railgun Optimal Range increased by 4km, Tracking Speed increased by 0.2.
Capital Rifled Railgun Damage increased by 15%, Activation Time increased by 5%, Optimal Range increased by 10km.
Small Strike Cannon Damage increased by 10%, Optimal Range increased by 0.95km, Accuracy Falloff increased by 0.25km.
Medium Strike Cannon Damage increased by 15%, Optimal Range reduced by 1.32km, Accuracy Falloff increased by 0.5km, Tracking Speed increased by 20%.
Large Strike Cannon Damage increased by 10%, Optimal Range reduced by 2.64km, Accuracy Falloff increased by 1km.
Capital Strike Cannon Damage increased by 10%, Activation Time reduced by 6.25%, Optimal Range reduced by 4km, Accuracy Falloff increased by 10km.
Reduced Large Missile Launcher Activation Time by 3.26%.
Increased Capital Missile Launcher Damage by 5%.
Increased Capital Beam Laser Damage by 10%.

Repair Amount, Activation Time and Capacitor Need for Large Armor Repairers reduced by 20% accordingly.
Capital Armor Repairer Repair Amount reduced by 10% and Activation Time reduced by 20%.

Medium Remote Shield Booster Repair Amount increased by 10%.
Large Remote Armor Repairer Repair Amount increased by 10%.
Capital Remote Armor Repairer Capacitor Need reduced by 10%.
Small Remote Capacitor Transmitter Capacitor Need increased by 50%.
Increased Small Armor Link Module Range to 10km, changed damage transfer to 60%, Self Armor Resistance increased by 16% upon activation (take Metalevel 8 as an example).
Increased Medium Armor Link Module Range to 20km, changed Damage Transfer to 70%, Self Armor Resistance increased by 13% upon activation.
Increased Large Armor Link Module Range to 30km, changed Damage Transfer to 80%, Self Armor Resistance increased by 9% upon activation.

Other Modules
Increased Small Energy Neutralizer Capacitor Need by 20%.
Reduced Medium Energy Neutralizer Capacitor Need by 10%.
Reduced Large Energy Neutralizer Capacitor Need by 5%.
Removed Explosion Radius and Explosion Velocity Bonus for Omnidirectional Tracking Link, gain 1/3 of its effect when not activated.
Drone Navigation Computer acceleration effect raised by 100%, gain 1/16 of its effect when not activated.
Reduced Flight Velocity Bonus for Fighter Microwarpdrive Guide to around 250%, Signal Radius Penalty changed to 250%, gain 1/24 of its effect when not activated.
Large Microwarpdrive, which currently has an anomalous Capacitor Need lower than the Afterbuner, hence the following adjustment： Increased the Capacitor Need by 50%, which is 10% higher than the Large Afterburner.

Drones
Current features for Drones are high in Flight Velocity, low in Orbiting Speed, and Weapon Tracking Velocity, which performs as follows in battle：
Out of Range - High-speed Tracking - Slow Orbit - Out of Range - High-speed Tracking… (loop)
This allowed Drones to actually do far less damage than they are supposed to, thus increasing the Tracking Velocity and decreasing the Flight Velocity in general.
Relevant Adjustments：
Increased Small Drone Tracking Velocity by 200% and reduces its Flight Velocity by 50%.
Increased Medium Drone Tracking Velocity by 150% and reduces its Flight Velocity by 30%
Increased Large Drone Tracking Velocity by 100% and reduces its Flight Velocity by 10%
The orbiting speed for the Fighter is increased to the same as its Flight Velocity (due to the specialized nature of the Fighter Heavy Rocket, it does not need to slow down for aiming during combat)
Other Adjustments：
Sentry Drone EHP raised by 50%.
Changed Optimal Range for Large, Medium and Small Drones to 4.8 / 4.8 / 2.4km.

Guard Optimal Range increased by 12km, and Tracking Velocity raised by 0.0084.
Templar Damage increased by 3.7%.
Einherji Damage increased by 4.8%, Accuracy Falloff reduced to 5km.
Acolyth, Warrior, flight velocity -5%
Infiltrator, Valkyrie, flight velocity -10%
Praetor, Berserker, flight velocity -10%

With the rise of productivity across New Eden, CONCORD has opted to remove prototype modules and trial SKINs while adding more practical reward items such as Lazarus Units.

LP Store Update
The LP Store plays a very important role in capsuleer training. As the Sleeper threat continues to grow, CONCORD has authorized access to more items.

Assault Drones
These drones are used in assault operations. They have high flight velocity, long weapon range, and great accuracy, but have weak defenses. They're suited for circling or chasing down enemy capsuleers at range, and are an effective counter to anti-drone pulse bombs.

Offense Drones
These are heavy drones designed to attack targets at close range. They have low weapon range, high attack power, and relatively good defense, which makes them an ideal choice if you want more firepower. However, their low flight velocity renders them powerless against fast targets and makes them easy prey for large enemy ships.
These drones also come in small, medium and large sizes. You can exchange their blueprints at the LP Store to manufacture them.

Anti-Drone Pulse Bomb
Faced with the growing threat of drones, researchers at New Eden have developed a new weapon. It can disrupt the communication channels of surrounding drones, causing them to overheat and damaging their circuits.
Due to the high energy cost, this type of weapon can only be fitted on a high slot. They're usually used by battleships. Please note that these weapons impact any drones indiscriminately, so exercise caution when friendly drones are nearby.
You can exchange their blueprints at the LP Store to manufacture them.

Nanocore Supply Chest
To make it easier for capsuleers to get their desired nanocores, CONCORD has removed the original nanocores and loaded them into supply chests for capsuleers to choose from.
