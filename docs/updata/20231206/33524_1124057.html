Patch Notes 1206

2023-12-06

Dear pilots of New Eden,

EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 12/6/2023 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

 

New Content

Capital Ship Component Manufacturing Skills

Master the systems and structural design of different capital ship components, reducing waste and improving production efficiency through process improvement and standardized operations, thereby reducing the ISK spent on producing capital ship components.

Capital ship components are classified into Weapon, Defense, Power, and other systems. Due to the significant technical differences between different systems, training in different skills can only reduce the manufacturing price of their corresponding types of capital ship components.

How to obtain: Obtain through the Industrial Skill Lucky Chest in the CONCORD Pass Shop (Advanced and Expert skills are not available yet)

Tips: This type of skill has low versatility but a strong influence, suitable for capsuleers who want to specialize in capital ship manufacturing

 

Balance Update

Capital Ship Component Manufacturing Skills have been added to the Industrial Skill Lucky Chest in the CONCORD Pass Shop.

Increased the chance of obtaining Filaments when opening Scan Loot Crates.

 

Nanocore Update

Silver Waltz series Core can now be upgraded to AI Core.

 

Optimizations

Valuation updated based on the market transaction record.

 

Bug Fixes

Fixed the issue where the scan resolution bonus does not falloff when multiple Signal Amplifiers are installed.
