Ban Notice for 03/12-03/19

2021-03-22

Here are details regarding accounts banned between March 12 and March 19 that exhibited anomalous behavior which violates our Terms of Service. This is broken down based on the situation the account violations were taking place under.

Abnormal Encounter Behavior: Accounts that exhibited bannable behavior while attempting Encounters.
Abnormal Mining Behavior: Accounts that exhibited bannable behavior while mining.
Abnormal Anomaly Behavior: Accounts that exhibited bannable behavior while attempting general anomalies.
Abnormal Transaction Behavior: Accounts that had exhibited bannable behavior while buying/selling on the market.

Accounts Banned: 57,336
Breakdown of Violations Per Banned Account
Abnormal Encounter Behavior - 11.16%
Abnormal Mining Behavior - 3.30%
Abnormal Anomaly Behavior - 80.71%
Abnormal Transaction Behavior - 4.83%
