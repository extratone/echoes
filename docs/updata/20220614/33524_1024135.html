Patch Notes - June 15

2022-06-14

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 6/15/2022 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

New Content
Upgraded UI of New Eden Store
Spike the Magnate has overhauled the New Eden Store for the Interstellar Bazaar. Three new sections, "Bestseller Combo," "Regular Sales," and "Exchange Store," have been added. The visible area of the product details has been expanded to bring a better shopping experience to all Capsuleers.

Illusionary Ghost Nanocore
Exclusive to Interstellar Bazaar, the "Illusionary Ghost" Nanocore comes with stunning glitch art and provides unparalleled attribute bonuses. In addition to that, it also provides an engaging music visualizer and dazzling special effects for your ship. Fit this Nanocore to your ship immediately, and you'll make New Eden glamorous tonight!

New Song: Enigmatic Blues
Spike the Magnate has invited Miss Iristar, a famous musician from New Eden, to produce a new song named Enigmatic Blues for the Interstellar Bazaar. The unique retro and psychedelic style goes well with the bustling night scene of the metropolis.

System Distance Calculator
To make it easier for Capsuleers to plan jump routes, a distance calculation function has been added to the Star Charts page. A system selected can be simulated as the jump start or end point, and the real-time distance will be shown.

Jump Clone
In response to the growing demand for rapid assembly and travel, CONCORD has authorized the launch of the jump clone. Upon registering at a Cloning Center and spending some ISK, Capsuleers can immediately create a jump clone in a station in another system.
After cloning, you will enter a state of fatigue which takes 48 hours to recover. You can learn skills to reduce the recovery time or purchase nerve relief services to quicken the recovery.
There are Cloning Centers in Capsuleer Outposts and Corporation Outposts, but the permissions to dock and use them are required for registration and cloning.
Capsuleers can only register for one Cloning Center initially. Learning skills can increase the max limit and reduce the cost of cloning. However, the maintenance cost of linking multiple Cloning Centers will increase exponentially. CONCORD reminds the Capsuleers to register the number of Cloning Centers as needed.

New Skills
Learning the Infomorph Synchronizing skill, which can be purchased with ISK, can reduce jump clone recovery time and cloning cost as well as increase the max limit of registered Cloning Centers.

NEW EVENT

The Interstellar Bazaar opens again as the Interstellar Exhibition kicks off

Interstellar Exhibition
Event Duration: 00:00:00 on June 16, 2022 - 00:00:00 on June 26, 2022
Empires in New Eden are in an increasingly fierce arms race. A new series of capital ship nanocore were introduced to Capsuleers: the llusionary Ghost. Capsuleers will have the chance to try out all capital ships of different factions through simulated battles and gain tons of LP and abundant rewards. Obtain 3 Interstellar Exhibition Lucky Chests by completing 3 Interstellar Exhibition games every day. Open Lucky Chests to participate in the lucky draw of the day.

Interstellar Bazaar: Neon Jungle
Event Duration: 00:00:00 on June 16, 2022 - 00:00:00 on July 16, 2022
The Interstellar Bazaar is around the corner! Mr. Spike (the event's curator), Miss Iristar (a well-known singer), and Mr. Kaizer (a modern artist) hope that you can assist in preparing the event. As the preparation progresses, Capsuleers can get abundant ISK rewards from preparation missions and unlock tons of IPs, Lazarus Units, and precious Interstellar Bazaar Commemorative Medals.

Interstellar Bazaar: Lucky Chart
Event Duration: 00:00:00 on June 16, 2022 - 00:00:00 on June 26, 2022
Obtain Luck by opening Lucky Chests in the New Eden Store, daily mails, and Interstellar Exhibition. The total Luck gained each day will be counted in the Lucky Chart.
Capsuleers will be rewarded according to their rankings on the Lucky Chart. For details about the reward settings, please refer to the rules on the event page.

Interstellar Bazaar: Lucky Chest
Event Duration: 00:00:00 on June 16, 2022 - 00:00:00 on June 26, 2022
Lucky Chests have been added to the New Eden Store, and they contain the new Illusionary Ghost Nanocore. Open Lucky Chests for a chance to win the lucky prize of the day. Discount codes can be used in the event for even greater value. You can get 1 Lucky Chest every day during the event by logging in.
Special Note: Most daily rewards of Lucky Chests come from the insurance purchases during the previous Pop Art Exhibition - Caille's Night.
List of lucky prizes: carriers of the four empires, faction battleships, Deadspace modules, integrated rigging modules, etc. Lots of benefits are coming.


Interstellar Bazaar: Interstellar Offering
Event Duration: 00:00:00 on June 16, 2022 - 00:00:00 on June 26, 2022
When Luck obtained from the Lucky Chest reaches the specified thresholds during the event, capsuleers will obtain bountiful rewards such as skill points, Neon Rain Capsule SKIN, general nanocore materials, and Neon Rain Capital Ship Opt Box.

Interstellar Bazaar: Interstellar Bonus
Event Duration: 00:00:00 on June 16, 2022 - 00:00:00 on June 26, 2022
During the event, capsuleers can unlock the PLEX refund privilege by spending AUR. The refund rates of PLEX will be based on the capsuleer's total spending during the event.
Refund Currency Type: PLEX
Refund Amount: total AUR spent during the event * corresponding refund ratio.
Refund Threshold: 499 AUR. No refunds below 499 AUR.
Max Refund Limit: up to 150,000 PLEX in this event.
All refunded PLEX will be issued to capsuleers by mail after the event. The event page data will only display the statistics.

Interstellar Bazaar: Starry Carnival
Event Duration: 00:00:00 on June 16, 2022 - 00:00:00 on June 26, 2022
All Corporation members can unlock the option bonus for the entire Corporation when the accumulated Luck obtained from the Lucky Chest during the event reaches the specified thresholds. Every capsuleer in the Corporation can join the fun and share the rewards. NPC Corporation will not participate in this event.

Inscription of the Galaxy: Cosmic Dust
Event Duration: 00:00:00 on June 25, 2022 - 00:00:00 on August 31, 2022
The giant fell in silence. The new season Cosmic Dust is about to kick off. Please view the in-game event description for more details.
