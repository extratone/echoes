Patch Notes - Nov 30

2022-11-29

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 11/30/2022 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

New Content
Overall Enhancement for Combat Special Effects
In order to recreate the ever-changing battlefield environment, we have carried out a comprehensive optimization of special effects, hoping to meet the high expectations of Capsuleers.
The following content has been optimized:
High Slot Modules: Miner, Strip Miner, Remote Shield Recharger, Remote Armor Repairer, Laser, Cannon, Railgun, a variety of Missiles and Capital Weapons.
Mid Slot Modules: Stasis Webifier, Warp Disruptor, Warp Scrambler, Sensor Dampener, Tracking Disruptor, Target Painter, Guidance Disruptor, Energy Nosferatu, Energy Neutralizer, Remote Capacitor Transmitter, Command Burst, Interdiction Sphere and Warp Disruption Field Generator.
Outer Space: Blocking Effect for star luminosity, station decorations, station display icons, partial post-processing effects, ship trailing effect, skybox effect, navigation lights for Trading Center.

Optimizations
1. The estimated price has been updated based on the market transaction record.
2. Added an easy feature for Planetary Production to automatically launch all yields.
3. Optimized the fuel system of structures. Now, structures such as the Resource Extraction Management Center will prioritize the use of fuel reserves, and also be able to use items from fuel tanks.
4. Optimized the alert when there is insufficient fuel.
5. The "Refuel" button has been added to the circular operation menu that pops up when you tap the ship. When tapped, it will attempt to search for cargo hold fuel and quickly fill it.
6. Upgraded the range of Sentry Guns to 2,500 km.
7. Optimized the speed of opening the ship's cargo hold when there is too much wreckage around. It now only displays up to 10 closest wrecks and chests within 10 km.
8. Updated the image of the login reward button.

Bug Fixes
1. Fixed the issue where customer support messages sometimes would not be sent.
2. Fixed the issue where some of the Nanocores or Supply Chest icons in the LP Store were incorrect.
