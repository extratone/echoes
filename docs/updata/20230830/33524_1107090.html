Patch Notes 0830

2023-08-30

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 8/30/2023 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

New Event
Nanocore Collection Guaranteed Reward Rotation
Update Time: After 2023.8.30 08:00 (UTC +0)
The guaranteed reward for Nanocore Collection has been updated. Opening up to 60 times will grant you the Thermomagnetic Storm & Ascension Battleship Nanocore Opt Box.

New Arrivals at Trailblazer Supply Depot
Sleeper series Nanocores with “Sleeper Mode” return for a limited time. They are now available for purchase again at Trailblazer Supply Depot.
Sleeper Mode: When enabled, Capacitor Recharge Time -75%, All Damage Resistance +50%, Damage -25%, Shield Recharge and Armor Repair -25%. It lasts for 30 seconds with a 300-second cooldown
Note: The Sleeper Mode and the Special Mode of the ship do not interfere with each other and can take effect at the same time

Inscription of the Galaxy: Strife
The Inscription of the Galaxy: Strife Season Final Ranking will be announced publicly at 08:00 (UTC+0) on August 30, 2023
Public Announcement Time: 08:00 August 30, 2023 - 08:00 September 13, 2023 (UTC+0)

Optimizations:
Valuation updated based on the market transaction record.

Bug Fixes:
Fixed an issue with the incorrect effect of Remote Armor Repairer Rigs.
Fixed an issue where some unstable wormholes might not be passable.
