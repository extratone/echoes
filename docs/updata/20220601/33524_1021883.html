Patch Notes - May 31

2022-05-31

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 6/1/2022 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.
 
NEW EVENT
Navigator Schedule
Event Duration: June 2 to July 2, 2022
The Navigator Program is on again! Top up a total of 499 AUR to start a new round of monthly Skill Points replenishment. Top up to a certain amount of AUR for additional rewards. Please refer to the in-game descriptions for details.
 
Optimizations
1. Updated estimated prices based on successful transactions in the Market.
2. Search feature is enabled when limiting Corporation Mission candidates.
3. Both ISK and Points Management Permissions need to be authorized when Corporation Mission Permission is authorized.
4. Enabled the Corporation Mission Sharing feature.
5. Removed Add-on Structures in Corporation Mission Locations.
 
Bug Fixes
1. Fixed the issue where there is a lack of PVP content in CONCORD Pass.
2. Fixed the editing issue of the Corporation Mission input box.
3. Fixed the issue where the Role Settings Screen in Permission Management couldn't be dragged up or down.
