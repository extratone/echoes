Patch Notes 0521

2025-05-21

To New Eden Capsuleers,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 5/21/2025 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

New Content
Capital Ship Rig II
With the advancement of Capital Ship rigging technology, the Capital Ship Rig II is now available for manufacture and use.
How to obtain (Regular): Use Type I Blueprints, Datacores, and Rigging Experiment Data to perform reverse engineering and obtain Type II blueprints, then manufacture with them.
Tip: Capital Ship Rigging Experiment Data can be obtained from loot crates from Capital Ship Nihilus Space and Capital Ship Proving Grounds.

New Integrated Rig
4P Combat Integrated Module + and 4P Engineering Integrated Module + are now available for manufacture and use.
Attribute Efficiency: 80%
Multiplier of Rigs Need: 12
How to obtain (Regular): Obtain blueprints (rare drops) by scanning the highest-level Relics, then manufacture with them.
How to obtain (Limited Time): Purchase from the Store's Hot Selection, which is available for a limited time.
Suggestion: Due to the high number of rigs required for integration and the fact that insurance only covers the integrated rig itself, it is not recommended to use it in large-scale battles where destruction is likely.

Integrated Rig Precise Dismantling Service
You can now use the Precise Dismantling Service to revert integrated rigs back to their unintegrated state. All integrated rigs will be fully returned to the station hangar.
Due to the complexity of the process, using the Precise Dismantling Service requires a service fee (ISK).
This service is suitable for dismantling Advanced Rigs. For basic rigs, it is recommended to use the existing standard dismantling and packing service.
Tip: Integrated rigs can only be dismantled individually. When selecting multiple items for dismantling and packing, integrated rigs will be automatically ignored.

New Advanced Implant Unit
Five new Advanced Implant Units have been introduced, as follows:
Pulse Crystal Capacitor Reinforcement
An advanced unit for Pulse Crystal that reinforces the ship capacitors. Reduces weapon power consumption during skill activation. Increases the ship's maximum capacitor capacity. Reduces the shield percentage consumed by Reverse Power.
Barrage Repression Firing Reinforcement
An advanced unit that reinforces Barrage Repression firing capabilities. Increase cannon tracking speed. Reduce Barrage Repression restart time. Increase Suppressive Fire duration and enhance the velocity decrease effect.
Thermal Circulation Power Control Reinforcement
An advanced unit for Thermal Circulation that strengthens power control systems. Increase power gain from Salvo. Increase the power cap, but power now decreases faster after overheating. Reduce restrictions on Overdrive skill activation.
Warhead Charge Warhead Reinforcement
An advanced unit for Warhead Charge that enhances warhead-specific effects. Reduce Precision Warhead damage and further decrease its explosion radius. Reduce Range-extending Warhead damage penalties. Increase damage bonuses for the four single-attribute warheads.
Aerospace Tactics Precision Reinforcement
An advanced unit that enhances the precision of aerospace tactic system. Improve drone tracking speed and range. Reduce the explosion radius of fighters.
Due to the variety of implant types, advanced units for other implant types will be introduced later.
How to Obtain:
Purchase from the Store, which is available for a limited time.
It is expected to be added as drops in Malpais in two weeks.

Brand-new Returnee Mission System
The Returnee System has been revamped and upgraded. Returning Capsuleers can obtain generous rewards through modules such as basic tutorial reviews, 7-day missions, returnee sign-ins, and update guide, helping them reacquaint themselves with New Eden.
Added a basic review mission module to help returning Capsuleers quickly revisit basic content in New Eden. Capsuleers familiar with New Eden can skip some of the missions and claim rewards right away.
Added three types of returnee buffs to assist Capsuleers in improving their capabilities.
1. Repair costs for returnee Capsuleers are reduced by 100%.
2. Rewards for completing Encounters missions as a returnee Capsuleer are increased by 20%.
3. Manufacturing time for items is reduced by 10%.
Added the update guide, allowing returning Capsuleers to quickly learn about the primary gameplay and event content currently available in New Eden.
Consolidated and optimized the 7-day returnee missions.
1. Optimized some of the missions. When the 7-day missions are first generated, they will recommend gameplay missions based on the Capsuleer's returnee questionnaire choices. Capsuleers can also change to other types of mission through refresh.
2. Reduced the number of 7-day missions that can be completed daily, from 5 to 4.
3. Optimized the rewards for 7-day missions. The Aeon Shards reward for each mission is standardized to 400, and the daily max limit of Aeon Shards obtainable has been increased from 1500 to 1600.
Optimized and increased the overall rewards of the returnee system.

Recommended New Arrivals
Opening Time: May 21, 2025, 08:00 - June 11, 2025, 08:00 (UTC+0)
Type IV Integrated Module + Draw Supply is now available in the Store. Upon opening, there is a chance to obtain Type IV Integrated Module + Opt Supply (includes Engineering and Combat Rigging Integrated Modules).
New Unit Draw Supply is now available in the Store. Upon opening, there is a chance to obtain the new Advanced Units and Reactive Implant Units.
The Soaring Dragon Series Nanocore returns. Soaring Dragon Opt Supply (Battleship) and Marshal Soaring Dragon Core Supply are now once again available in the Store. Opening the Soaring Dragon Draw Box for a chance to obtain Capital Ship Soaring Dragon Nanocore Opt Supply.

Ship Balance Adjustments
Anubis. Missile velocity bonus adjusted from +220% to +170%. Missile flight time reduction adjusted from -50% to -40%.
All Yan-Jung Faction ships are now equipped with Sun Chaser Engines by default, allowing jumps within the Empire via Star Charts. (In the future, the warm-up time for jumps in Hi-Sec and Low-Sec areas will be significantly reduced, and more Sun Chaser Engine-related technologies will be introduced)
Navy Issue Carrier. Fighter bay capacity increased by 20,000 cubic meters.
Versatile Assault Ship. New bonus: Capacitor Warfare Resistance +25%.
Anaconda. Lightweight Ship turret damage increased to match rocket damage, with rocket range bonus remains unchanged.
Astarte. Lightweight Ship rocket damage increased to match turret damage, with turret tracking speed bonus remains unchanged.
Villain. Lightweight Ship rocket damage increased to match turret damage.

Market Adjustments
PLEX sales tax rate increased from 2% to 5%.

Cosmic Anomaly Adjustments
The loot crate from the highest level Relics may now contain the 4P Integrated Module + Blueprints (rare drops).
The loot crate from Capital Ship Covert Rift and Capital Ship Proving Grounds may now contain the Capital Ship Rigging Experiment Data, used for reverse engineering the Advanced Rig blueprints.

Construction Adjustments
Capital Ship Rig I. Reduce the manufacture technology level requirement from Lv.10 to Lv.9.
Standard Rig IV Blueprint. Reduce the technology level requirement for reverse engineering from Lv.10 to Lv.9.
Crawler Neural Compiler. Reduce the manufacture technology level requirement from Lv.5 to Lv.4.

Optimizations
Optimized the logic of AI Combat Core. If no combat targets are found, it will quickly attempt to locate new targets.
To enhance fairness, fleet creation of any form is prohibited in regions such as Azure Dome, and fleet sharing is also disabled.
Optimized the introduction of certain gameplay features.
In Malpais, the trigger time for space radiation damage has been extended to 2 hours.

Capital Ship Soaring Dragon Nanocore Visual Optimization
Capital Ship Soaring Dragon Core's visual optimization, with a new standby animation added to the holographic dragon projection.

Bug Fixes
Fixed an issue where the team restriction feature was ineffective in the Kalevala Expanse in certain conditions.
Fixed an issue where the AI Combat Core might automatically shut down under certain circumstances.
Fixed the abnormal data in the old version of the Gravity Well Trap, with its usage count now reset every Monday.
Fixed an issue where Cosmic Anomalies in Malpais would drop Kalevala Expanse Needlejack Filaments.
Fixed an issue in Malpais where the description of space radiation damage effects was incorrect.

Strengthened Anti-Cheat Penalty Announcement
To create a fairer gaming environment and protect Capsuleer interests, the customer service has strengthened the anti-cheat penalties. Capsuleers who identify suspicious individuals can report them to the customer service.
Overview of Four Common Penalty Cases:
Individuals found using cheats to earn bounties or mine will face an initial penalty of 20-40 billion ISK, depending on the impact and duration of use. Continued violations after the first penalty will result in significantly increased penalties, with severe cases leading to permanent bans.
If more than 10 members of the same Corporation are found using cheats, the Corporation will be temporarily restricted from trading, and a portion of its ISK will be deducted.
If more than 20 members of the same Corporation are found using cheats, and the total number of active members is relatively low, the Corporation will be permanently shut down.
Using automated surveillance cheats to monitor local channels will result in a permanent ban.
