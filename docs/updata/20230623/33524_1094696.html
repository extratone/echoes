Important Notice Of The Interstellar Bazaar

2023-06-23

Dear Pilots:
We are very sorry, due to the wrong data configuration, the Luck Value of the ships in Interstellar Bazaar in the first three days is abnormal.
The Interstellar Bazaar has been in operation for 2 days. After receiving the BUG feedback, we immediately verified and discussed the solution, and urgently repaired the problem. Now we will announce the abnormality and compensation to all pilots.

Abnormal situation
The lucky ships in the first three days of the Interstellar Bazaar will get an extra 5,000 lucky points when they get the flagship; they will get an extra 2,000 lucky points when they get the battleship. These two values are the values of the previous Interstellar Bazaar half a year ago, and the event this time is set to 500 and 400.
After the previous Interstellar Bazaar event held half a year ago, we received suggestions from some pilots. After discussion, we agreed that the total amount of rewards for winning the lucky prize (Ships and Daily Ranking Prize) should be reduced, and instead Rewards are distributed to more pilots to reduce the chance of winning both ship awards and ranking awards. Therefore, in this event, the lucky values ​​of ships have been reduced to 500 and 400. As of the time of repair, the number of people affected was approximately 24.

Solution
Based on the principle of being fair and just to all pilots and minimizing the loss of pilots, we believe that no additional treatment will be given to pilots who have won the ship award. All lucky values, lucky ships, and lucky cores remain the same.

Compensation plan
1. Leaderboard Compensation
Due to the abnormal luck value of the ship, some pilots' luck values may be falsely high, resulting in the failure of those who might have been ranked. We believe that these pilots should also be rewarded.
First of all, for pilots who have already placed in the top 5 in the leaderboard for the first two days, their ship core rewards remain the same.
Then, we excluded these 5 pilots, substituted the lucky value of the new version of the ship to recalculate the total lucky value of all pilots, took out the top 5 of each day again, and presented an additional supercarrier nano core self-selection box.
More feedback is welcome.

2. Full server compensation
We apologize for the anomaly that affected the experience of all pilots participating in the Interstellar Bazaar, and hereby prepare 10 high-density nanobodies, 30 amethyst cones, 200K SP and 2 million skill reset carriers for all pilots in the service. These will be distributed after the next maintenance, so please stop by any space station as soon as possible to pick them up.

Thank you for your support of EVE Echoes. We will continue to process all relevant pilot feedback and comments to bring you a better experience of the event.
