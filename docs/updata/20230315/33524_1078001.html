Patch Notes - MAR 15

2023-03-15

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 3/15/2023 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

Optimizations
1. The estimated price has been updated based on the market transaction record.

Bug Fixes
1. Fixed the issue where Salvagers were less effective when salvaging multiple wrecks.
2. Fixed the issue where the Voyage Nanocore Micro Eject Mode was less effective when used in conjunction with Afterburners.
3. Fixed the issue where low-tech level players were incorrectly restricted from piloting high-tech level ships in Faction Wargames.
4. Fixed the issue where the damage bonus for Bombard Tactics was not removed in some scenarios when durability was depleted.
5. Fixed the issue where Capsuleers were unable to use their Award Vouchers in the Personal Citadel and Corporation Citadel.
