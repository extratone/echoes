Patch Notes - June 8

2022-06-08

Dear pilots of New Eden,
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 6/8/2022 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

NEW EVENT
The final rankings of Inscription of the Galaxy: Burning Night will be publicized on the event page from June 10 to June 17.
 
Optimizations
1. Updated estimated prices based on successful transactions in the Market.
2. Added a new limit for joining a Corporation. Each Corporation can accept up to 200 people per day.
3. Now pilots can earn Combat Commendation times by logging in every week.
4. The Rig Blueprints in the Market Sidebar are divided into 3 categories: weapon, defense, and engineering rig blueprints.
5. Added the guide for Road to Set Sail to Training Tutorials.
6. Added the feature to stack and batch submit the same collection in the Museum Arcana.
 
Bug Fixes
1. Fixed an issue where the Damaged ORE Ship Hull could not be traded.
2. Fixed an issue where drones would not orbit at optimal range.
3. Fixed an issue where the rate and bonus skill were incorrect when doing reverse engineering for the Industrial Module Blueprint.
