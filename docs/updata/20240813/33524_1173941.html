Patch Notes 0814

2024-08-13

To New Eden Capsuleers, 
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 8/14/2024 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance. 

Balance Update
Inquisitor Cosmic Anomaly. Increases discovery rate by 10% (relative to the original rate. Same applies to the followings).
Regular Nihilus Space. Increases discovery rate by 20%.
Etherium Reach's Wasteseeker Striker Fleet appears more frequently, with increased waves, shortened total defense time, and a 100% increased chance of dropping Drone components.

New Event
Voyage Ceremony Registration is Open
Registration Time: August 15, 2024, 00:00:00 - August 20, 2024, 00:00:00 (UTC +0)
The bustling New Eden Starport was filled with anticipation as people held their breaths. Corporation fleets arrived one after another at the site of the Voyage Ceremony. Once again, before setting off for the new world, they proudly formed their ranks and stood in formation under the watchful eyes of the masses, ready to write their own legends.
Capsuleers are required to participate in the event as Corporations, with a total of 36 slots available. The top three Corporations on the Inscription of the Galaxy S8 leaderboard will be invited, along with 12 randomly selected Corporations from the top three of each S1 to S7 season.
Qualified Corporations can register themselves, and the list of successfully-registered Corporations will be confirmed and published after the random screening.
Please check the Voyage Ceremony Event page for more information.

Voyage Ceremony Formation Modification
On August 20, all corporations that successfully sign up for the Voyage Ceremony will be announced, and the Corporation Formation Modification will be enabled on the event page. Corporation Administrators: Please edit formations and assign members before the deadline on the event page.
After modification, please tap “Save and submit” on the “Preview” page as soon as possible and submit the Corporation Fleet Formation to the server.

7-Day Login
Event Duration: August 14, 2024, 08:00 - August 21, 2024, 08:00 (UTC+0)
Log in every day during the event to claim generous rewards.

The Lost Wasteseeker Fleet Returns
Event Duration: August 14, 2024, 08:00 - September 4, 2024, 08:00 (UTC+0)
Many Wasteseeker Fleets have appeared across various systems. CONCORD, after observation, suspects these are just the Wasteseeker's vanguard, with an unknown crisis possibly lurking in the shadows, ready to strike.
Defeating Wasteseekers gives a chance to obtain special modules of Metalevel 5~7, Drone components, and a small number of rare Fighter Blueprints (Shrike and Red-tailed Hawk).

Anniversary Celebration
Event Duration: August 14, 2024, 08:00 - September 4, 2024, 08:00 (UTC+0)
In collaboration with the major empires, CONCORD launches the Celebration Reward Event. During the event, Market Transaction Tax rates are reduced, the Planetary Production Yield is increased, and the number of items dropped from defeating pirates is increased.

Insurance System Adjustment
Adjusted the Free Insurance Points feature, making it identical to regular Insurance Points, where both are capable of creating Market Orders.

ISK Exchange Store Optimization
Lowered the price of most exchange items.

Optimizations
Optimized the description of Sentry Drones and Damage Control Units among other items

Bug Fixes
Fixed an issue where the Rorqual could not be delivered to Low-Sec Trading Centers
Fixed an issue with the wrong number of Mid Slots on the Maelstrom
Fixed an issue where the Tempest Striker was missing one Mid Slot
