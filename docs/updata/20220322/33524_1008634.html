Patch Notes - March 23

2022-03-22

EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 3/23/2022 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.

New Content
New Item: Lazarus Unit
The Lazarus Unit is a sample made from scanning and analyzing the skill area of the Capsuleer brain. It also used a small amount of DNA extracted from their spinal fluid. Using the unit can increase the quota for resetting skill points.
Special Reminder: In the upcoming April balance patch, CONCORD will send free Lazarus Units to Capsuleers according to their tech level, so don't miss out!

Corporation Recruitment Improvements
Several recruit features were improved to help pilots better recruit for their corporations:
1. Share Corporation
A share button has been added to the Corporation Business Card and Corporation interface. Tap the button to share a templated simple Corporation profile to in-game channels or other apps, along with a QR code for players to scan and find your Corporation.
2. Better Direct Invitation
A new recruit feature has been added to the Corporation Members interface, where Corporation Managers can search for players meeting their needs, and directly send invitations.
*Pilots can disable the feature in settings so their name will not appear in search results.

New Eden Store Updates
1. Added 2 new novice capsuleer supplies to the page, including special ships, exclusive Nanocores, etc.
2. Added Lazarus Unit packs to the page.

New Event
New Eden Recruitment Event
Event Duration: March 23 - May 29
Gather comrades and find your place in the universe with New Eden Recruitment Event! Obtain rewards by recruiting fresh pilots and rallying veteran ones. Help these players complete event missions to get  Recruiter Coins, Skill Points, Insurance Points, Will of Steel Nanocore Opt Boxes and Honor Medals. Find out more about the rules in the game.

Capsuleers Return
Event Duration: after the March 24 update
Continue the infinite journey in New Eden with this upcoming event! Apart from the exclusive 8-day login rewards for Returnees. You can also obtain Aeon Shards by completing missions for 7 days. With Aeon Shards, you will be able to obtain Returnee Supplies in the New Eden Store. Exclusive sale packs are also available! Find out more about the rules in the game.

Navigator Program
Event Duration: March 24 - April 24
The Navigator Program is on again! Top up a total of 499 AUR to start a new round of monthly Skill Points replenishment. Top up to a certain amount of AUR for additional rewards. Please refer to the in-game descriptions for details.

Inscription of the Galaxy: Burning Night
Event Duration: March 23 - May 29
Two new leaderboards [Munition Rankings] and [Spark Rankings] are added. Please refer to the event page for more information.

EVE Echoes Corp Propaganda Poster Contest
Event Duration: March 28 - May 13
Participate in the Corp Propaganda Poster Contest by posting your original recruit posters on Facebook/Twitter.
Attract new recruits and win up to 2,000 PLEX and Time Series SKIN Opt Box. Please refer to the event page for more information.

Optimizations
1. Updated estimated prices based on successful transactions in the Market.
2. Comets will soon visit New Eden. Capsuleers in all systems will have the chance to witness comets streak across the skies.
3. If the first event in the event page has sub-pages, it will now automatically unfold.
4. Updated the reminder text when repackaging Nanocores.
6. The report page has now been added. There are now report buttons in Alliances Business Cards, Corporation Mails and Citadel pages.
7. The Store has been updated. The Splendid Coupon rewards are no longer available. Item Purchases will no longer come with Coupons. The Coupon Store will close on March 30. By that time the coupons will expire. Please exchange them before the store closes.

Bug Fixes
1. Fixed the issue where "gift claimed" notices won't show up when claiming more than 9 packs in New Eden Store.
2. Fixed the issue where some Nanocores appear in Splendid Coupon Store.
3. Fixed the issue where Hurricane and Drake Neon Gas Cores may appear as drop items.
