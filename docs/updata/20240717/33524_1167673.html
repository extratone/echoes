Patch Notes 0717

2024-07-17

Dear pilots of New Eden, 
EVE Echoes will undergo maintenance on 7/17/2024 at 08:00 (UTC+0) and will take approximately 120 minutes to complete. During this time, players will be unable to log in to the game. We kindly ask for your understanding and patience during the maintenance.  

New Event
EVE Echoes × Super Mecha Champions Collab Event
Opening Time: July 17, 2024, 08:00 - August 7, 2024, 08:00 (UTC+0)
The romance of space and mecha: when the high-octane excitement of Super Mecha collides with the expansive universe of EVE Echoes, an unprecedented collaboration event is about to begin!

Super Mecha Champions Collab Store
Opening Time: July 17, 2024, 08:00 - August 7, 2024, 08:00 (UTC+0)
New items include Super Mecha Champions - Assault Module Shipping Container, Super Mecha Champions - Integrated Module Shipping Container, Super Mecha Champions - Blueprint Shipping Container, Super Mecha Champions - Structure Skin Shipping Container, etc.
General Units Drone Lucky Draw Box, Heavy Fighter - Siege Modification Supply, Fighter Refactoring and Lightweight Ship Refactoring Supply restocked.

Super Mecha Champions Collab Drones/Fighters
Drawing on data from the Super Mecha Champions competition, military corporations from various countries have developed the latest mecha-type drones. These new drones and fighters boast significantly enhanced performance compared to conventional models.
How to obtain: Store purchase, industry manufacture.
During the event, there is a chance to obtain blueprints by purchasing Equipment Transport Crates from the Collab Store, and they also have a chance to drop from Cosmic Anomalies such as Wasteseeker Checkpoints and Wasteseeker Maps.
This collaboration includes 8 types of drones/fighters, each with 3 variants for capsuleers to choose from:
Large Drone - Hurricane
A modified model based on the experimental data of the highly agile combat mecha, using a modified drone rocket system, while still retaining its original advantages in agility and firepower.

Sentry Drone - Pulsar Sentinel
A modified model based on the experimental data of the dual-form combat mecha. The Pulsar in its cannon form possesses high firepower. By compromising its propulsion power to charge the plasma pulse cannon in its transformed form, it deals immense electromagnetic and thermal damage.

Shield Repair Logistic Drone - Caramel Heavy Cannon
This logistic drone is a modified model based on the defensive mecha, equipped with military support AI systems and shield maintenance devices. Its rounded bear head design reflects the designer's preference for a cute style.

Armor Repair Logistic Drone - Bolide
This is a modified model based on the industrial robot, equipped with military support AI systems and armor maintenance devices. In its original design, it could retract its body into a spherical shape to pass through complex environments or slam into obstacles.

Light Space Superiority Fighter - Pulsar Light
The light humanoid form of the Pulsar possesses extreme agility and retains the plasma pulse rifle as its primary weapon, capable of rapidly tracking targets and inflicting electromagnetic and thermal damage.

Light Attack Fighter - Pulsar Heavy
The heavy humanoid form of Pulsar sacrifices some agility in exchange for increased firepower from its plasma pulse rifles. This allows it to track targets more swiftly and deal greater electromagnetic and thermal damage.

Heavy Attack Fighter - Hot Steel
Modified from the experimental data of the heavy combat mecha. Although its heavy armor and massive main weapon grant it excellent defense and formidable firepower, the heavy artillery equipped on its right arm can unleash thermal, kinetic, and explosive damage on targets.

Lightweight Ship - Sky Dome
Modified from the experimental data of the air combat mecha. Equipped with a tailored anti-air high-speed rocket launcher, it can effectively engage aerial targets and has considerable burst firepower.

Super Mecha Champions Collab Structure Skin
It will be available for a limited time in the Collab Store during the event, including three skins: Super Mecha Champions - Jaka, Super Mecha Champions - Joanna, and Super Mecha Champions - Ning, covering both Capsuleer Outposts and Corporation Outposts. The skins will include exterior and interior effects for the structures.
Capsuleers have a chance of obtaining a Capsuleer Outpost Skin Opt Box or a Corporation Outpost Skin Opt Box by purchasing the Super Mecha Champions - Structure Skin Secret Trafficking Container from the store.
Note: After the Corporation Structure Skin is activated, the ownership of the Skin belongs to the Corporation. If the Capsuleer who purchased the Corporation Structure Skin leaves the Corporation, they will lose the authority to use that skin.

Super Mecha Champions Collab Event Encounters
Opening Time: July 17, 2024, 08:00 - August 8, 2024, 0:00 (UTC+0)
Added new Encounter Missions of Normal difficulty and above. During the event, capsuleers can accept collab Encounter Missions from the Encounters panel.
There is a small chance of triggering the Combat Event Encounter "Cleaner". Completing the Expert difficulty Encounter "Cleaner" can earn you a Collab Mission Assignment.

Mecha Frontline: Event Manual
Opening Time: July 17, 2024, 08:00 - August 7, 2024, 08:00 (UTC+0)
Mecha Frontline: Event Manual is now available!
Complete designated missions to get awesome rewards. Unlocking the manual will also grant extra rewards such as the Super Mecha Champions - Assault Module Opt Box, Super Mecha Decryptor, and Cosmic Treasure Opt Supply.
Cosmic Treasure Opt Supply: Based on feedback from capsuleers that high-value items have low freedom of choice, this supply allows capsuleers to choose from various valuable items, including encrypted supplies such as CyanSea, Lancer Dreadnought, and Lucent-Moon, as well as the Neon Gas Capital Ship Nanocore Lucky Draw Box.

Navigator Program
Event Duration: July 17, 2024, 08:00 - August 4, 2024, 08:00 (UTC+0)
The Navigator Program is on again! Top up a total of 499 AUR to start a new round of monthly Skill Points replenishment. Top up to a certain amount of AUR for additional rewards. Please refer to the in-game descriptions for details.
Premium Navigator Program: With the Anniversary Celebration approaching, only 360 AUR is needed to unlock the Premium Navigator Program, allowing you to claim Double Skill Points every day.

Capital Remote Capacitor Transmitter
A capital-class fleet support module that can transfer the ship's own capacitor charge to a remote target, suitable for ships with high capacitor capacity.
How to obtain: Manufacture them based on the blueprints purchased from the market.

Balance Adjustments
New Siege Reinforcement Tactical Modules can be purchased from the Jita Market. Activation time: 15 seconds, fuel usage: 150GJ, other attributes are the same as Lancer Dreadnought's Siege Mode II.
All Dreadnoughts and Lancer Dreadnoughts have had their built-in Siege Mode removed. They now have an additional mid slot (+1) and a new bonus: the ability to fit Siege Reinforcement Tactical Modules.
Logistic Support Tactical Module new effect: Remote Capacitor Transmitter transmission effect +50%.
The Minokawa: Powergrid +9% and added a new bonus: Remote Capacitor Transmitter range +75%, transmission effect +5% (per level).
The Lif: Powergrid +32% and added new bonus: Remote Capacitor Transmitter range +50%, transmission effect +7.5% (per level).
The Apostle: Powergrid +26% and added a new bonus: Remote Capacitor Transmitter range +75%, transmission effect +10% (per level).
The Ninazu: Powergrid +8% and added a new bonus: Remote Capacitor Transmitter range +100%, transmission effect +7.5% (per level)
Lucent-Moon: Powergrid +20% and added a new Bonus: Remote Capacitor Transmitter range +100%, transmission effect +10% (per level)
Added 10,000 Warp Jamming for Corporation Outpost, preventing any warp commands.

Cosmic Anomaly Adjustments
Scout Cosmic Anomalies will no longer appear in any systems. Instead, they will be replaced by Large Anomalies and Nihilus Space.
All rewards from Scout Cosmic Anomalies have been added to the Inquisitor Cosmic Anomalies of the same faction and level (one battle, double rewards).

Optimizations
Bomber battleships are now compatible with old series nanocores of the same type.
Added donation requirements to the Corporation Insurance Points description: Tech level of at least 7 and joined the Corporation for at least 30 days.
Removed volume information for Implants and Skills from item descriptions.
Removed Classified II Gravitational Wave Graph from the Shard Exchange store, rewards will continue to be optimized in the future.

Bug Fixes
Fixed an issue where the name and slots of a level 1 outpost were still displayed after upgrading the Corporation Outpost.
Fixed an issue where modifying structure names might fail.
Fixed an issue where combat modules on a Capsuleer Outpost might display as offline.
Fixed an issue where the cargo hold usage display on the home screen might always show 0%.
Fixed an issue where the visual size of some fighters was too small for its volume.
Fixed an issue where the market interface might not close properly when there are insufficient Insurance Points or PLEX.
