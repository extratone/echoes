Build Capital Ships Contest Coming Soon!

2022-09-27

As the New Eden's super ships, the Capital Ships are essential weapons in battles, revealing how strong the Corporations truly are. On September 29th, the brand new Capital Ships Jump Freighter and Force Auxiliary will be available. Build Capital Ships Contest will begin as well!
 

Two leaderboards will be introduced at the event: Best Quantity (Corporations) and Top Speed (Individual). Corporations or individuals who outperform in the contest will receive fabulous rewards including Medals, CONCORD Passes, and so on! We can't wait to see the first Corporation build the new Capital Ships and be on top of the leaderboards!

Event Rules:

Best Quantity: Corporations Ranking Leaderboard. To win the contest, participating Corporations must build as many Jump Freighters and Force Auxiliaries as possible within a limited time period. If the total number of ships built is identical to the other Corporations', the first Corporation that completes the last Jump Freighter or Force Auxiliary wins. Corporations with the highest total number of Jump Freighters and Force Auxiliaries will be on the leaderboard.

 

Top Speed: Individual Ranking Leaderboard. To win the contest, participating Pilots must build their Jump Freighter or Force Auxiliary as fast as possible within a limited time period. Pilots who are the fastest to build their ships will be on the leaderboard (NPC Corporations members are welcome to join).

 

1）Ships List:

Jump Freighter: Rhea, Nomad, Ark, Anshar

Force Auxiliary: Minokawa, Lif, Apostle, Ninazu

 

2) Event Duration:

Sept. 29th, 2022 - Nov. 10th, 2022, 23:59:59. (UTC+8)

It is expected that the results will be announced on November 16. The publicity period will be one week, and then the rewards will be distributed.

Completion time = Time to Start Building + Expected Time Spent, has nothing to do with the final completion time, but canceled ships are not counted

 

3）Best Quantity：The top 20 Corporations with the highest total number of ships built will be rewarded.

Top Speed：The top 8 Pilots who build the above 8 ships first will be rewarded.

 

4) Best Quantity Rewards will be issued to the CEO of the winning Corporations, who will distribute the prizes to the members according to their contribution to this contest.

Top Speed Rewards will be issued to the corresponding Pilots on the leaderboard.

 

5) Top Speed Leaderboard Details:

The leaderboards for different ships are mutually exclusive. If Pilot John wins first place in the Minokawa leaderboard and eighth in the Lif leaderboard, John will receive the rewards from both leaderboards.

Each player on the leaderboard for one ship can only receive a reward. If Joey is the first Pilot who builds the first three Minokawas, Joey will receive the first-place reward. The Pilot who builds the fourth Minokawa will take second place.

*Please note that the ranking is based on "Build Start Time + Spend Time".


6) The Award Code is valid until December 31st, 2022.

 

Rewards:

Best Quantity (Corporations):

No. 1: "Chief Capital Ship Engineer" Commemorative Medal *15 + 30-day Omega Duo Bundle *60 

No. 2: "Senior Capital Ship Engineer" Commemorative Medal *10 + 30-day Omega Duo Bundle *40

No. 3: "Capital Ship Engineer" Commemorative Medal *7 + 30-day Omega Duo Bundle *20

No. 4-20: "Capital Ship Engineer" Commemorative Medal *5 + 30-day Omega Duo Bundle *10

 

Top Speed (Individual):

No. 1: "Chief Capital Ship Engineer" Commemorative Medal *10 + 30-day Omega Duo Bundle *10

No. 2: "Senior Capital Ship Engineer" Commemorative Medal *7 + 30-day Omega Duo Bundle *7 

No. 3: "Capital Ship Engineer" Commemorative Medal *5 + 30-day Omega Duo Bundle *5 

No. 4-8: "Capital Ship Engineer" Commemorative Medal *3 + 30-day Omega Duo Bundle *3 


Actively Participation Award:
During the event, Pilots will receive a "Capital Ship Engineer" Commemorative Medal Activation Code for every Jump Freighter or Force Auxiliary they build. Multiple medals are available to claim.
